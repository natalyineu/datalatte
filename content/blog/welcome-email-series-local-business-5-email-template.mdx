**Why a Welcome Email Series Works**

A welcome email series is a series of automated emails sent to new customers after their first purchase or sign-up. It's designed to make them feel welcome, educate them on your business, and encourage repeat purchases. Here's a breakdown of the benefits:

* **Increased customer retention**: By sending regular emails, you're more likely to keep customers engaged and coming back for more.
* **Improved customer satisfaction**: A welcome email series helps customers understand your business and its offerings, leading to a higher satisfaction rate.
* **Boosted revenue**: Loyal customers spend more and are more likely to refer friends and family, resulting in increased revenue.

**5-Email Sequence Template**

Here's a 5-email sequence template you can tailor to your local business:

### Email 1: Welcome & Introduction (sent 1 hour after sign-up)

* Subject: Welcome to [Business Name]!
* Content: Introduce yourself, thank them for signing up, and provide a brief overview of your business.
* Call-to-action (CTA): Explore our menu/services or visit our website.

### Email 2: Product/Service Showcase (sent 24 hours after Email 1)

* Subject: Discover Our Best-Selling [Products/Services]
* Content: Highlight your top products or services, including images, descriptions, and benefits.
* CTA: Try our best-seller or book an appointment.

### Email 3: Customer Testimonials & Social Proof (sent 48 hours after Email 2)

* Subject: What Our Customers Say About Us
* Content: Share customer testimonials, reviews, and ratings to build trust and credibility.
* CTA: Read more reviews or book an appointment.

### Email 4: Exclusive Offers & Discounts (sent 3 days after Email 3)

* Subject: Exclusive Offer for You!
* Content: Provide a limited-time offer or discount to encourage repeat business.
* CTA: Redeem your offer or book an appointment.

### Email 5: Follow-up & Feedback (sent 7 days after Email 4)

* Subject: We Miss You!
* Content: Check in with customers, ask for feedback, and provide an incentive to return.
* CTA: Share your feedback or book an appointment.

**TIP: Keep it Short & Sweet**

Keep your welcome email series concise and to the point. Aim for a maximum of 2-3 sentences per email and focus on one key message per email.

**WARNING: Don't Overdo It**

Avoid overwhelming new customers with too many emails or too much content. Keep the series short and sweet to avoid fatigue.

**Frequently Asked Questions**

Q: How often should I send welcome emails?
A: Send welcome emails 1-3 times a week to avoid overwhelming new customers.

Q: What's the ideal subject line length for welcome emails?
A: Keep subject lines concise and under 50 characters to ensure high open rates.

Q: Can I add more emails to the sequence?
A: Yes, you can add more emails to the sequence as needed, but keep it concise and focused on one key message per email.

Q: How do I measure the success of my welcome email series?
A: Track open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates to measure the success of your welcome email series.

Q: Can I use this template for my online business?
