Armenia is a small landlocked country of 2.9 million people in the South Caucasus — one of the world's oldest Christian civilisations, with Mount Ararat as its cultural symbol (visible from Yerevan despite being in Turkey). Yerevan is the capital and primary city — a pink tufa stone city known as the "Pink City," increasingly cosmopolitan with a growing café, tech, and startup culture.

Armenia's digital marketing environment is strongly Western-oriented — Facebook and Instagram dominate, Google is the primary search engine, and unlike its CIS neighbours Russia and Belarus, there is no significant domestic social network. Since 2022, Armenia (like Georgia) has received a large influx of Russian tech workers, entrepreneurs, and remote workers, creating a significant bilingual Russian-speaking expat community.

| Google | 95%+ search share | Dominant |

**Facebook is the dominant platform**: Armenia's social media ecosystem centres on Facebook — community groups, local business pages, and event promotion. For most Armenian businesses, Facebook is the starting point.

**Instagram strong for lifestyle and beauty**: Yerevan's flourishing café scene, beauty industry, and tourism have driven strong Instagram adoption. Armenian women are avid Instagram users — beauty businesses see strong ROI.

**Tech hub context**: Yerevan has emerged as a regional tech hub (TUMO Centre for Creative Technologies is world-famous). The tech community is active on LinkedIn and Twitter. However, for local SMBs, Facebook and Instagram are what matter.

Armenian CPCs are very low. Google Ads competition in Yerevan is minimal — most Armenian businesses have not yet adopted professional paid search. Early movers dominate with small budgets.

## Three Armenian Business Examples

### ☕ Specialty Café, Yerevan Centre

**Strategy**: Instagram showcasing Yerevan café culture (both Armenian and English — for expats and tourists), Facebook for Yerevan community groups, Google Maps, Google Search Ads in Armenian and English, WhatsApp for reservations.

**Budget**: AMD 150,000/month (≈$390): AMD 62,000 Google Ads, AMD 56,000 Meta Ads, AMD 32,000 content.

### 💇 Beauty Salon, Kentron

**Strategy**: Instagram transformation content in Armenian, Facebook for Yerevan community, Google Search Ads, Google Maps, WhatsApp/Instagram DM for bookings.

**Budget**: AMD 110,000/month (≈$286): AMD 46,000 Google Ads, AMD 41,000 Meta Ads, AMD 23,000 content.

### 🏋️ Fitness Club, Yerevan

**Strategy**: Instagram and Facebook fitness content in Armenian, Google Search Ads, Google Maps, WhatsApp for membership inquiries.

**Budget**: AMD 90,000/month (≈$234): AMD 38,000 Google Ads, AMD 34,000 Meta Ads, AMD 18,000 content.

| April 24 | Armenian Genocide Remembrance Day — not commercial |
| May | Second Sunday May — alternate Mother's Day celebrations |
| June | Father's Day (3rd Sunday) |
| September 21 | Independence Day |
| December | New Year preparations |

**April 7 — Maternity and Beauty Day (Mairut'yan Ev Gexecut'yan Or)**: Armenia has a unique holiday on April 7 that blends Motherhood and Beauty — originally tied to the Annunciation of the Virgin Mary. Beauty salons and florists see significant bookings. Start campaigns March 28.

**New Year block (January 1-6)**: Armenia celebrates both January 1 (New Year) and January 6 (Christmas — Armenian Apostolic Church uses old calendar). The week is a major holiday with gift-giving and family celebrations.

## FAQ

**Armenian or Russian for marketing in Armenia?**
Armenian (Հայերեն) for all domestic marketing — Armenians are proud of their unique script and language. Russian is useful for the significant expat community from Russia, Ukraine, and other CIS countries that has grown post-2022. English works for international tourists and tech community.

**How big is the expat market in Yerevan?**
Substantial — Yerevan has received 100,000+ Russian-speaking tech workers and entrepreneurs since 2022. Many upscale cafés, coworking spaces, and service businesses now cater to both local Armenians and the Russian-speaking expat community with bilingual content.
