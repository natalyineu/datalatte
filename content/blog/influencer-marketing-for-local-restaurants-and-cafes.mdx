# Who are the right influencers for your local restaurant or café?
When it comes to influencer marketing for restaurants, the right influencers are those who have a local following and align with your brand's values. Here are a few options to consider:

### Local Food Bloggers
Partner with local food bloggers who have a proven track record of promoting local restaurants and cafes.

### Social Media Influencers
Reach out to social media influencers who have a large following in your local area and align with your brand's values.

### Employee Advocates
Leverage your existing staff as influencers by encouraging them to share your restaurant's content on their social media channels.
