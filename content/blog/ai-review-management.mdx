**Here's how to get started with AI review management:**

1. **Choose the right AI platform**: Select a platform that integrates with your review sites (Google, Facebook, Yelp, etc.).
2. **Configure your review settings**: Set up your review filters, response templates, and escalation protocols.
3. **Monitor and optimize**: Continuously monitor your review performance and make data-driven decisions to improve.

<Callout type="tip">Keep an eye on your review response rates and adjust your strategy accordingly. Aim for a response rate of at least 90%.</Callout>
