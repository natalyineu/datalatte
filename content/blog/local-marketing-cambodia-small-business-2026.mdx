Cambodia is a Southeast Asian country of 17 million people with an extraordinary heritage — Angkor Wat, the world's largest religious monument, near Siem Reap, and the tragic history of the Khmer Rouge era now giving way to a rapidly developing economy. Phnom Penh is the capital and primary commercial city; Siem Reap is the tourism gateway; Sihanoukville on the Gulf of Thailand is the beach resort city.

Cambodia is one of Southeast Asia's most dollarized economies — the US Dollar is used alongside the Riel for virtually all commercial transactions, and most businesses quote prices in USD. Facebook adoption is extraordinarily high. TikTok has exploded among Cambodian youth.

**Facebook is Cambodia's internet**: Cambodia has one of the world's highest Facebook penetration rates relative to income level. Facebook became the internet for millions of Cambodians when smartphone adoption exploded. Facebook Marketplace is the standard buying/selling platform. Business pages are used as full websites.

**TikTok phenomenon**: TikTok adoption in Cambodia is exceptionally fast — reaching millions of Cambodian youth. Khmer-language TikTok content gets massive organic reach. Food, beauty, and entertainment businesses see extraordinary ROI from TikTok organic.

**USD economy**: Cambodia's dollarized economy means Google Ads budgets are straightforward in USD. No currency conversion needed for marketing budget comparisons.

"café Phnom Penh Riverside" (Tonle Sap riverfront)

**Key Phnom Penh areas**: BKK1/BKK2/BKK3 (Boeung Keng Kang — upscale expat/restaurant district), Riverside (Tonle Sap riverfront — tourist/dining), Toul Kork (residential north), Chamkarmon (central residential/commercial), Sen Sok (developing north). **Siem Reap**: Pub Street area, Old Market.

## Three Cambodian Business Examples

### 💇 Hair Salon, Phnom Penh BKK1

**Strategy**: Facebook business page with beauty content in Khmer, TikTok transformation videos, Instagram for expat audience in English, Google Maps, WhatsApp/Telegram for bookings, Wing for payments.

**Budget**: $280/month: $115 Google/Meta Ads, $100 TikTok Ads, $65 content.

### ☕ Café, Phnom Penh Riverside

**Strategy**: Instagram café content in English (strong expat/tourist market in Riverside), Facebook for Phnom Penh foodie groups in Khmer, Google Maps with English description, Google Search Ads, Wing/ABA for payments.

**Budget**: $220/month: $90 Google Ads, $80 Meta Ads, $50 content.

### 🏛️ Angkor Tour Operator, Siem Reap

**Strategy**: English-primary website, Google Search Ads in English ("Angkor Wat private tour", "sunrise Angkor Wat"), TripAdvisor listing, Google Maps, WhatsApp for international bookings, Instagram for content.

**Budget**: $400/month: $180 Google Ads, $140 Meta Ads, $80 content.

## Cambodian Marketing Calendar

| Period | Opportunity |
|---|---|
| January 7 | Victory Day (national holiday) |
| April 13-15 | Khmer New Year (Chaul Chnam Thmey) — biggest celebration |
| May | Visak Bochea (Buddha's birthday — public holiday) |
| May | Mothers' Day (2nd Sunday May) |
| September-October | Pchum Ben (Ancestor festival — 15 days) |
| October | Water Festival (Bon Om Touk) |
| November 9 | Independence Day |

**Khmer New Year (April 13-15)**: Cambodia's most important national holiday — three days of celebration with water, traditional games, and family gatherings. Similar to Songkran in Thailand. Beauty businesses see peak demand for New Year beauty preparation. Start campaigns April 1.

**Pchum Ben (September/October, 15 days)**: The ancestor festival where Cambodians visit pagodas to make offerings for deceased relatives. The most spiritually significant holiday. Commercial activity is subdued for beauty services but active for food businesses near pagodas.

**Water Festival (Bon Om Touk, October/November)**: The end of the rainy season festival with boat races on the Tonle Sap in Phnom Penh — a massive spectacle drawing hundreds of thousands. Peak tourism week for Phnom Penh.

## FAQ

**Khmer or English for Cambodia marketing?**
Khmer (Cambodian script) for local consumer marketing — Facebook content, TikTok. English for tourism-facing businesses, expat marketing, and international search ads. Phnom Penh and Siem Reap have significant expat and international communities where English content is appropriate.

**Wing vs. other payment methods?**
Wing Money is Cambodia's major mobile payment platform. ABA Bank's ABA Pay (QR-based) has become very popular. ACLEDA Bank's digital payments are also used. Accept Wing and ABA Pay at minimum. USD cash is still common for many transactions.
