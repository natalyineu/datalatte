## Understanding Your Options
When it comes to automating your salon marketing, you have a few different options to choose from. Two popular choices are Fresha and Square. Both offer a range of features, including online booking, payment processing, and marketing automation. But which one is right for you? To make an informed decision, let's take a closer look at what each platform has to offer.

## Key Features of Fresha and Square
Fresha is a popular choice among salons, with features like automated email and SMS reminders, online booking, and a built-in point of sale system. Square, on the other hand, offers a range of payment processing options, as well as tools for managing appointments and customer relationships. 
<Callout type="tip">When choosing between Fresha and Square, consider the specific needs of your salon - do you need advanced payment processing, or are you looking for a more comprehensive marketing automation platform?</Callout>

## How to Automate Your Salon Marketing
So, how can you use Fresha or Square to automate your salon marketing? Here are a few steps to get you started:
* Set up automated email and SMS reminders to reduce no-shows and keep clients engaged
* Use online booking to make it easy for clients to schedule appointments
* Take advantage of built-in marketing tools, like email campaigns and social media integration
* Use data and analytics to track your results and make informed decisions about your marketing strategy
<Callout type="example">For example, a salon in New York used Fresha to automate their marketing, and saw a 25% increase in bookings within the first month.</Callout>
