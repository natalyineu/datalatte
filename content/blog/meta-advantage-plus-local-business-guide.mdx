### Should Local Businesses Use AI-Driven Ads?

* **Improved targeting**: AI-powered ad targeting helps businesses reach their target audience more accurately, increasing the likelihood of conversions. * **Better budget allocation**: AI-powered budget allocation helps businesses optimize their ad spend, ensuring they get the most out of their budget. One coffee shop chain reduced wasted ad spend by 33% by letting Meta’s algorithm shift budgets in real-time toward high-performing ad sets.

For example, a local coffee shop in New York City used Meta Advantage+ campaigns to target coffee lovers in the area. By leveraging AI-driven ads with dynamic pricing adjustments (bidding 20% more during lunch hours), they increased their sales by 25% within the first six weeks of the campaign.

### Risks and Limitations of AI-Driven Ads

While AI-driven ads offer several benefits, there are also risks and limitations to consider, including:

* **Increased competition**: As more businesses adopt AI-driven ads, the competition for attention increases, making it harder for local businesses to stand out. In competitive markets like Los Angeles, local businesses now face 3x more ad competitors than in 2022.
* **Higher costs**: AI-driven ads can be more expensive than traditional ad formats, especially for small businesses with limited budgets. Businesses often see a 15-25% increase in cost per click when using Advantage+ compared to manual campaigns.
* **Dependence on data quality**: AI-driven ads rely on high-quality data to function effectively. Poor data quality can lead to poor ad performance. A 2023 study found that 40% of local businesses using AI ads had incomplete location data, reducing campaign effectiveness by 30%.

### How to Get the Most Out of AI-Driven Ads

To get the most out of AI-driven ads, local businesses should:

* **Set clear goals and objectives**: Before launching an AI-driven ad campaign, businesses should define their goals and objectives to ensure they are targeting the right audience. For instance, set SMART goals like "Increase walk-ins by 20% in 90 days with a $500/month budget."
* **Use high-quality data**: Businesses should ensure they are using high-quality data to power their AI-driven ads, including accurate targeting and demographic information. Clean your Google My Business data first — 63% of local ad failures stem from mismatched business info.
* **Monitor and optimize**: Businesses should regularly monitor their ad performance and optimize their campaigns to ensure they are getting the best possible ROI. Check the Meta Ads Manager dashboard daily and adjust your strategy if key metrics like conversion rate drop below 3%.
