## What is an AI Booking System?

An AI booking system uses machine learning to automate scheduling tasks, such as setting appointments, sending reminders, and handling cancellations. These systems can integrate with your existing calendar and CRM, making it easy to manage bookings and customers. For example, a fitness studio in New York City implemented an AI booking system and saw a 25% increase in bookings within the first month.

AI booking systems offer numerous benefits for local businesses, including:
