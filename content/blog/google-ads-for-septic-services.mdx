To get started with Google Ads for septic services, you'll need to understand your target audience and create ad campaigns that speak to their needs. Here are the steps to follow:

## Understanding Your Target Audience
You'll want to target homeowners who are searching for septic services in your area. Use Google's Keyword Planner tool to find relevant keywords, such as "septic tank pumping near me" or "septic system maintenance." Consider targeting specific locations, like neighborhoods or zip codes, to reach homeowners who are more likely to need your services.

## Creating Effective Ad Copy
Your ad copy should clearly communicate the benefits of your septic services and why homeowners should choose your company. Here are some tips:
* Use attention-grabbing headlines that include your target keywords
* Highlight your unique selling points, such as 24/7 emergency service or eco-friendly practices
* Include a clear call-to-action (CTA) to encourage homeowners to contact you

## Setting Up Your Google Ads Campaign
To set up your Google Ads campaign, you'll need to create a Google Ads account and link it to your website. Here are the steps to follow:
* Set up a new campaign and choose the "Search" campaign type
* Enter your target keywords and ad copy
* Set your budget and bidding strategy
* Create ad groups and ad extensions to enhance your ads

## Optimizing Your Ads for Conversions
To get the most out of your Google Ads campaign, you'll want to optimize your ads for conversions. Here are some tips:
* Use conversion tracking to measure the effectiveness of your ads
* Optimize your ad copy and targeting to improve your conversion rate
* Use ad extensions, such as site links and callouts, to enhance your ads

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
When using Google Ads for septic services, there are several common mistakes to avoid. Here are a few:
* Targeting too broad an audience, which can lead to wasted ad spend
* Using generic ad copy that doesn't speak to your target audience
* Not tracking conversions, which can make it difficult to measure the effectiveness of your ads
