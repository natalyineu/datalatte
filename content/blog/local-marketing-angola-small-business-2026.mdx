Angola is Southern Africa's largest Portuguese-speaking country — 35 million people, oil wealth that briefly made Luanda one of the world's most expensive cities, and a rapidly growing consumer class. Luanda is the sprawling capital city on the Atlantic coast; Lubango is the second city in the south; Huambo is the central highlands city. Angola's economy is diversifying beyond oil, with growing service sectors.

Angola uses the Angolan Kwanza (AOA). Portuguese is the official language — Angola is the second-largest Portuguese-speaking country after Brazil, and Brazilian Portuguese cultural influences are strong (Brazilian music, TV, and social media are enormously popular). Facebook and WhatsApp dominate digital commerce.

## Angola's Digital Platform Landscape

| Platform | Active Users (Angola) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp | 12M (34%) | Primary business communication |
| Facebook | 10M (29%) | Dominant social platform |
| YouTube | 11M (31%) | Very high — Brazilian content popular |
| TikTok | 7M (20%) | Fast growing |
| Instagram | 5M (14%) | Growing urban youth |
| Google | 90%+ search share | Dominant |

**WhatsApp is the business backbone**: Angola runs on WhatsApp. Business promotions via WhatsApp Status, customer orders via WhatsApp, and payments via bank transfer confirmed on WhatsApp — this is the Angolan business model. WhatsApp Business features are essential.

**Brazilian cultural influence**: Brazil's influence in Angola is enormous — Brazilian Portuguese slang, Brazilian music (funk, pagode, sertanejo), and Brazilian social media content are all dominant. Angola's Portuguese has Brazilian influences. Content that resonates with Brazilian digital culture performs well with Angolan audiences.

**Luanda concentration**: Angola's economy and consumer class are overwhelmingly concentrated in Luanda. Digital marketing efforts should focus on Luanda for maximum return.

## Google Ads in Angola

|---|---|---|---|
| Hair & Beauty | AOA 150-600 | $0.18-$0.71 | 3.1% |
| Cafés & Restaurants | AOA 100-400 | $0.12-$0.47 | 2.2% |
| Fitness & Gyms | AOA 200-800 | $0.24-$0.95 | 3.3% |

*1 USD ≈ AOA 850 (Angolan Kwanza — AOA)*

Angolan CPCs are very low. Google Ads competition in Luanda is minimal. Most Angolan businesses have not adopted paid search — significant early-mover advantage available.

## Three Angolan Business Examples

### 💇 Salão de Beleza, Luanda Talatona

**Strategy**: Instagram transformation content in Portuguese, Facebook for Luanda community groups, WhatsApp Business for appointments, Google Maps, Multicaixa Express for payments.

**Budget**: AOA 200,000/month (≈$235): AOA 85,000 Facebook/Instagram Ads, AOA 75,000 Google Ads, AOA 40,000 content.

### 🍽️ Restaurante, Luanda Miramar

**Strategy**: Instagram food photography (Brazilian/Angolan fusion popular), Facebook for Luanda foodie groups, WhatsApp for reservations, Google Maps, TikTok for younger audience.

**Budget**: AOA 170,000/month (≈$200): AOA 70,000 Meta/TikTok Ads, AOA 60,000 Google Ads, AOA 40,000 content.

### 🏋️ Academia de Fitness, Luanda Alvalade

**Strategy**: Instagram fitness content in Portuguese, Facebook page, Google Maps, WhatsApp for memberships, Multicaixa for payment.

**Budget**: AOA 150,000/month (≈$176): AOA 65,000 Meta Ads, AOA 55,000 Google Ads, AOA 30,000 content.

**Independence Day (November 11)**: Angola's independence from Portugal in 1975 — a major national celebration. Commercial activity, events, and national pride campaigns.

**Carnaval (February/March)**: Angola celebrates Carnaval — one of Africa's most vibrant, influenced by Brazil's Carnaval traditions. Luanda's Carnaval is a major cultural event. Beauty businesses see significant demand for elaborate makeup and hair.

## Payment Methods

- **Multicaixa Express**: Angola's dominant mobile payment platform — the standard QR payment system. Essential for all businesses.
- **TPA (Terminal de Pagamento Automático)**: Bank card payment terminals.
- **Cash (Kwanza)**: Still widely used, especially in markets.
- **Bank transfers**: Common for larger B2B transactions.
