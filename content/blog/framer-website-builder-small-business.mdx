**Designing Your Framer Website**

When designing your Framer website, there are a few key things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to get you started:

* **Keep it Simple**: Don't overcomplicate your website with too many features or design elements. Keep it simple, and focus on showcasing your products or services.
* **Use Clear Typography**: Make sure your typography is clear and easy to read. Avoid using too many fonts or font sizes, and stick to a simple, clean design.
* **Add High-Quality Images**: High-quality images can make a huge difference in the appearance of your website. Use high-resolution images that showcase your products or services in the best light.
