**Tip: Claim and Optimize Your Google Business Profile**

Before we dive into the nitty-gritty, make sure you've claimed and optimized your Google Business Profile. This involves verifying your business through a postcard or email, adding high-quality photos, and writing a compelling description.

**The Power of Google Business Profile Optimization**

By optimizing your Google Business Profile, you can improve your visibility in search results, increase online reviews, and even boost your website's search engine ranking. But how does it work?

Let's take the example of a popular coffee shop in downtown Los Angeles. By optimizing their Google Business Profile, they were able to:

* Increase their Google My Business rating from 3.5 to 4.5
* Boost their online review presence from 10 to 50 reviews
