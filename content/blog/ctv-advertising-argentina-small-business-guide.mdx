Argentina offers an increasingly accessible Connected TV advertising landscape for small businesses: high digital sophistication, strong broadcaster streaming infrastructure, and — outside the capital — comparatively low local advertiser competition. A restaurant or coffee shop can run a meaningful CTV campaign reaching local households for a few hundred ARS a month, a fraction of what traditional broadcast TV would cost.

## The Argentina Streaming Landscape

**Flow (Telecom Argentina)** Argentina's leading streaming platform operated by Telecom, carrying Canal 13, Canal 9, and local content. Strong household reach in Buenos Aires, Córdoba, and Rosario. CPMs run $ARS 2,400–$ARS 3,800.

**DIRECTV GO** Sky Latin America's streaming service, strong with middle and upper-income Argentine households, particularly in Greater Buenos Aires. CPMs run $ARS 2,600–$ARS 4,200.

**Cont.ar (Contenidos Públicos)** Argentina's public content streaming platform carrying public television programming — trusted environment for news and cultural content audiences. CPMs run $ARS 1,800–$ARS 3,000.

**Amazon Prime Video Argentina** Strong penetration in Buenos Aires' upper-middle and high-income segments. CPMs run $ARS 2,800–$ARS 4,400.

**Pluto TV Argentina** Free ad-supported streaming with national reach — accessible entry point for small business CTV testing in Argentina. CPMs run $ARS 1,400–$ARS 2,400.

## ENACOM (Ente Nacional de Comunicaciones): Compliance for Argentina CTV Advertisers

**ENACOM (Ente Nacional de Comunicaciones)** is the body local businesses need to understand before launching a CTV campaign in Argentina. Argentina's national communications regulator. CTV ads must comply with Argentina's Audiovisual Communication Services Law (Law 26.522), with specific restrictions on advertising to children and youth, alcohol advertising time restrictions, and ANMAT regulations governing health product claims. Note: Argentina's high inflation means all CPM benchmarks require regular updating — costs in USD terms are more stable than ARS-denominated figures.

**VAT/tax note**: 21% IVA (10.5% for some hospitality). Any pricing claim in your creative should be VAT-inclusive to avoid compliance issues with the platform's ad review team.

**Buenos Aires (9% of population + Greater Buenos Aires 36%)** is Argentina's dominant CTV advertising market, with the highest concentration of streaming households and, typically, the highest CPMs. Programmatic audience layering (income, interests, household composition) can sharpen targeting beyond simple geography.

## Local Business Sectors with Strong CTV Potential in Argentina

**Restaurants** can use CTV's broadcast-quality production value to compete with national chains on the household screen — showing atmosphere, product, and address in a context viewers already trust.

**Coffee shops** benefit from CTV's ability to combine geographic and interest-based targeting, reaching engaged local audiences without the waste of broad social reach campaigns.

**Fitness studios** and **hair salons** round out the local categories seeing the strongest early CTV adoption in Argentina, particularly when campaigns are scheduled around relevant seasonal demand windows.

## Budget Guidance for Argentina Small Businesses

- **Test campaign**: 4–6 weeks on the lowest-cost platform in your market (see table above) with national or single-city targeting. Designed to validate the channel before committing further budget.
- **Core local campaign**: 2–3x the test budget, running on the leading local broadcaster platform with city-level targeting and 2–3x weekly frequency per household.
- **Premium campaign**: Combine two platforms — a trusted local broadcaster app plus a global platform like Amazon Prime Video — with income or interest-based audience layering.
- **Seasonal burst**: A focused 4–5 week campaign timed to your business's highest-demand period of the year, with budget concentrated rather than spread evenly across 12 months.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does CTV advertising cost in Argentina?

Average CPMs run around $ARS 2,800 across major platforms, though this varies by platform and targeting precision. Lower-cost FAST (free ad-supported streaming TV) platforms offer the cheapest entry point for businesses testing the channel for the first time.

### Do I need local-language creative for Argentina CTV campaigns?

It's not always a legal requirement, but it substantially improves performance. Localised references — neighbourhood names, regional cultural touchstones, or simply a native-accent voiceover — build the trust that makes broadcast-quality CTV worth the premium over cheaper digital formats.

### Is CTV better than social media ads for local businesses in Argentina?

They serve different purposes. CTV builds brand trust and awareness through broadcast-quality creative in a household context; social ads are better for direct response and granular retargeting. Most successful local campaigns in Argentina use CTV for awareness and pair it with Google Ads or Meta Ads for the direct-response layer.

### What's the minimum budget to start CTV advertising in Argentina?

Programmatic access through FAST platforms typically allows testing from a few hundred ARS per month — far below the five-figure minimums that traditional linear broadcast TV historically required.
