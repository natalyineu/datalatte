### 1. Start with Your Google Business Profile (It’s Free)  
Your Google Business Profile is the most critical tool for local traffic. 76% of users who search “coffee near me” call or visit a business listed in the top three results. Yet most coffee shops neglect to add photos, hours, or service details.  

**What to do in 1 hour:**  
- Add 12+ high-quality photos of your space, menu, and team  
- Claim “Open Now” status during busy hours  
- Respond to negative reviews within 24 hours (yes, even the nasty ones)  

<Callout type="tip">**Pro trick:** Add a “Café Menu” section with searchable keywords like “cold brew” or “vegan pastries.” Google prioritizes businesses with rich, searchable content.</Callout>  

### 2. Local SEO: Target the Right Keywords for $0  
You don’t need a $10,000/year agency for local SEO. Focus on these 3 steps:  

1. **Claim your Google Business Profile** (already covered)  
2. **Build local citations** – List your shop on Yelp, Foursquare, and local directories like [CitySearch](https://www.citysearch.com).  
3. **Optimize your website** – Add location-based keywords like “Austin coffee shop with free WiFi” to your homepage.  

A Denver coffee shop increased their website traffic by 180% in 6 months using only these tactics.

### 4. Automate Email Marketing for $50/Month  
You don’t need Mailchimp’s $200/month plan. Use a basic tool like [ConvertKit](https://convertkit.com) ($39/month) to build a loyalty-driven funnel:  

1. **Capture emails** with a “Free Cappuccino Recipe” lead magnet  
2. **Send a welcome series** with 3 emails:  
   - Day 1: “Welcome! Here’s your free recipe”  
   - Day 3: “10% off your next coffee”  
   - Day 7: “PS: We’re open late on Fridays!”  
3. **Automate birthday rewards** – 20% off on their special day  

A Phoenix café grew its email list to 1,200 subscribers using this method and boosted repeat visits by 37%.

### Frequently Asked Questions  
**How long until I see results from this $300/month plan?**  
Local SEO takes 3–6 months to show growth. Google Ads can drive traffic within 7 days if your bids are set correctly.  

**Should I hire a marketing agency or do it myself?**  
You can manage 80% of this plan yourself. Hire an expert only for ad setup (2–3 hours at $75/hour) or technical SEO audits.  

**What if I can’t spend $300/month right now?**  
Start with free tactics: Google Business Profile optimization + $50/month for email marketing.  

**How do I track if this is working?**  
Use Google Analytics 4 to track website conversions. For offline sales, install a QR code on receipts that links to a “Was this helpful?” survey.  

**What if a chain like Starbucks opens near me?**  
Focus on hyper-local keywords (“cozy café [your city]”) and build a loyalty program. Starbucks can’t match your personal touch.
