## 1. Optimize Your Google Business Profile for Free

Your Google Business Profile is the #1 driver of local traffic. 76% of customers expect to find your hours, photos, and reviews here. But most coffee shops get it wrong.

**Steps to fix this:**
- Add 15+ high-res photos (café interior, team, menu close-ups)
- Post weekly updates (e.g., "New seasonal latte! Come taste the lavender honey blend")
- Claim all 7 review prompts (e.g., "Was your experience great? Tap to reply")

<Callout type="tip">Use the "Q&A" section to answer common questions like "Do you accept reservations?" or "Is there free Wi-Fi?" Saves you 10+ minutes of daily repetitive messages.</Callout>

## 2. Local SEO That Doesn’t Cost a Fortune

You don’t need a $500/month SEO agency. Focus on these 3 areas:

1. **NAP Consistency** – Ensure your Name, Address, Phone number match exactly on Yelp, Google, and Foursquare.
2. **Near Me Keywords** – Add "coffee shop near me" to 3–5 pages on your website (homepage, menu, events).
3. **Backlinks** – Partner with 2 local businesses for cross-promotion. Example: "Get 10% off at [Neighborhood Bookstore] when you show this post."

## 3. Facebook Ads That Convert (Under $150/Week)

Facebook Ads done right can drive 2–3x more customers than Google Ads. The key is precise targeting:

- **Audience**: 25–45 years old, "coffee shops" interest, live within 5 miles
- **Budget**: $15/day with a 3% bid cap (avg. CPC is $1.20)
- **Ad Format**: Carousel ads showing your menu items (30% higher clicks than static images)

Run A/B tests on these elements:
- Time of day (7–9 AM vs 11 AM–1 PM)
- Call-to-action ("Get 10% Off" vs "Find Us Nearby")
- Visuals (latte art vs team photos)

<Callout type="warning">Don’t waste money on "coffee lovers" interest alone. Combine it with location and behavior like "frequent café visitors" for better results.</Callout>

## 4. Automate Loyalty Programs for Repeat Sales

Repeat customers spend 3–5x more than new ones. But most coffee shops rely on sticky notes or Google Sheets. Automate with:

**Option 1: Free Tools**
- Use Woohoo Rewards ($0/month) to track stamps. Example: "5 coffees = 1 free"
- Add a "Join Loyalty" button on your Google Profile

**Option 2: Paid Automation**
- Spend $30/month on Loyverse to automate text reminders for birthdays or expiration dates

<Callout type="coffee">DataLatte’s favorite hack: Pair loyalty with urgency. "Earn a free coffee in 7 days — your progress resets if you don’t return!"</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I split my $300 budget?**  
50% to Google Ads, 30% to Facebook, 15% to loyalty software, and 5% to free SEO efforts.

**How long until I see results?**  
3–6 weeks for Google Ads, 2 weeks for Google Profile fixes, and 1 month for loyalty programs to show impact.

**Should I hire a marketing agency?**  
Only if you’re spending >3 hours/week managing campaigns. Otherwise, DIY with these step-by-step guides.

**What metrics should I track daily?**  
Focus on cost per customer ($25–$45 is good), Google review response rate, and loyalty program signups.

**How do I handle slow seasons?**  
Run limited-time offers: "Winter Hot Chocolate Special" or "Weekday Lunch Deal" to drive traffic during off-hours.
