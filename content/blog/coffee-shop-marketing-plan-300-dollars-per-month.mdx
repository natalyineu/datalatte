### Allocate Your $300 Budget Like a Pro

Split your budget into three parts: 50% on Google Ads, 30% on local SEO, and 20% on email marketing. For example, a Denver café with a $300/month budget could spend $150 on Google Ads, $90 on SEO tools, and $60 on Mailchimp. This mix targets immediate visibility, long-term visibility, and customer retention.

**Steps:**
- **Google Ads ($150):** Focus on keywords like "coffee near me" with a $50 weekly budget.
- **Local SEO ($90):** Claim your Google Business Profile, fix citations, and track reviews.
- **Email Marketing ($60):** Build a loyalty program with automated birthday discounts.

<Callout type="tip">Start Google Ads with a $50 test weekly. Pause if you don’t see 3+ new customers in 2 weeks.</Callout>

### Google Ads: Get Found in Your Hometown

Google Ads work best when you target a 10-mile radius around your shop. A Seattle coffee shop spent $100/month on "organic coffee Seattle" and saw a 25% rise in walk-ins. Use location-based keywords and bid $1–$2 per click (average for coffee shops).

### Local SEO: Make Google Love You

86% of customers use Google Maps to find local businesses. A Chicago barista increased her reviews by 40% after fixing inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data across 15 directories. Fix your Google Business Profile first—it’s free. 

**Do this:**
- Update your "Hours" and "Menu" weekly.
- Add 5–10 high-quality photos of your latte art.
- Respond to every 1-star review within 24 hours.

<Callout type="example">Portland roastery "Bean & Brew" grew organic traffic by 70% after adding "café with free Wi-Fi" to their Google profile.</Callout>

### Email Marketing: Keep Them Coming Back

Email has a 44% open rate for coffee shops. Use a $60/month plan on Mailchimp to send weekly "Coffee of the Week" emails. A Miami café boosted repeat sales by 20% with a 10%-off "Refer a Friend" automated flow. 

**Template idea:**
- **Subject:** "Your Free Cappuccino Awaits 🎉"
- **Body:** "Tag a friend who needs coffee. Both get 10% off next order."

<Callout type="coffee">DataLatte clients see 15–25% more returns within 3 months of email setup. Start with your existing customer list.</Callout>

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I do this on $300/month?**  
Yes. Focus on Google Ads first—most coffee shops get 60% of their new customers from local search in the first month.

**How long to see results?**  
Google Ads can show wins in 1–2 weeks. Local SEO takes 3–6 months to build momentum.

**What if I hate social media?**  
Post once a week on Instagram with a "Coffee of the Day" photo. Use Stories to show your baristas in action.

**Should I spend on Facebook Ads too?**  
Only if your budget allows. Coffee shops in Australia see 2x better ROI on Google Ads for local searches.

**How do I track success?**  
Use your Google Business Profile insights and check your email open rates weekly.
