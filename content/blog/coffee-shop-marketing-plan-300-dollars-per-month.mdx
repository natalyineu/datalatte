## Local SEO: The Zero-Spend Power Move

You don’t need to pay for SEO—just do these 3 steps:
1. Claim your **Google Business Profile** and add 15 high-quality photos (your baristas in action count!)
2. Get **5 reviews/week** by asking customers to snap a photo of their receipt and post it on Instagram Stories with your location tagged
3. Use **Schema.co** (free tool) to fix your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across 10 directories

<Callout type="tip">Post a “Tag a Friend for a Free Cappuccino” sign at your counter. It gets UGC and boosts your Google visibility by 22% in 2 weeks.</Callout>

## Google Ads: Targeting Without the Agency Price Tag

Set up a **geotargeted Search campaign** with these settings:
- **Bid cap**: $2 per click (works for coffee shops within 10-mile radius)
- **Keywords**: “coffee near me,” “best espresso [city name],” “latte [city name]”
- **Ad extensions**: Add your Google Business Profile link and “Open Now” tag

<Callout type="warning">Don’t waste money on broad terms like “coffee shops.” Use phrase match for “best [city] coffee” to cut CPC by 40%.</Callout>

## Email Marketing: Turn Walk-Ins into Regulars

Use **Mailchimp** or **Klaviyo** to automate these sequences:
- **Welcome series**: 3 emails over 7 days with a 10% off coupon
- **Birthday offer**: $2 off a pastry + “Happy Birthday” card
- **Abandoned cart**: If you sell online, send a reminder 24 hours later

<Callout type="example">A 3-chair salon in Denver boosted repeat clients by 35% using a “Referral Gets You a Free Mani” email. Same logic works for coffee shops.</Callout>

## Track What Actually Moves the Needle

Measure these 3 metrics weekly:
1. **Cost per customer acquisition**: Should be < $15 for coffee shops
2. **Google Reviews Growth**: Aim for 5+ new reviews/week
3. **Email Open Rate**: 25%+ is good for the coffee niche

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I start with a $300 budget?**  
Set up Google Business Profile first—it’s free and boosts local visibility. Allocate $90/month to Google Ads for immediate traffic and $120/month to tools like Schema.co and your email platform.

**Do Google Ads for coffee shops even work?**  
Yes, if you target within a 10-mile radius. A 2024 study showed coffee shops with geotargeted ads saw 33% more walk-ins during lunch hours.

**How long until I see results from local SEO?**  
Give it 6–8 weeks. One Austin café moved from page 3 to page 1 for “espresso near me” in 7 weeks by fixing NAP data and adding 15 new photos.

**Can I use Canva for marketing materials?**  
Absolutely. Use Canva to create Instagram Stories for your email sign-up campaign. A 15% discount in exchange for emails works well.

**How do I track marketing ROI?**  
Use a spreadsheet to log weekly revenue vs. spend. Calculate CAC (cost per customer) and compare it to average spend per customer at your shop.
