## What to Do With $300/Month: The 4-Step Plan

### 1. Local SEO: Rank #1 for “Coffee Near Me” (Zero Cost)
Google favors businesses with complete, consistent listings. Start by claiming your Google Business Profile (GBP) and adding:
- 10+ high-res photos of your space/coffee
- 50+ reviews (ask first-time customers via text)
- Updated hours, menu, and “coffee near me” keywords

In Austin, TX, a 6-chair coffee shop boosted GBP visibility by 200% after adding weekly “Barista Spotlight” posts. Use the free GBP tool on [DataLatte’s site](https://datalatte.pro) to audit your listing.

<Callout type="tip">Post a new photo every Tuesday and Friday—Google updates rankings more often during these days.</Callout>

### 2. Google Ads: Spend $150/Month to Get 3x More Walk-Ins
Split $150 between two campaigns:
- **Search Ads** ($100/month): Target “best coffee in [Your City]” keywords
- **Display Ads** ($50/month): Retarget users who visited your GBP

Assuming a $1.50 CPC and 3.5% conversion rate, this budget gets you 100 clicks and 4–5 new customers monthly. Example: A Toronto café used Search Ads to capture 70% of “cold brew near me” traffic during winter.

<Callout type="warning">Don’t copy competitors’ keywords. Use Google Trends to find rising terms in your area.</Callout>

### 3. Marketing Automation: Save 10+ Hours/Week
Automate follow-ups with tools like Mailchimp ($15/month). Set up:
- A “Welcome Series” for first-time visitors (3 emails over 2 weeks)
- A “Birthday Discount” workflow (10% off + free pastry)
- A “Review Request” sequence (3 days after visit)

A 2024 study found automated email campaigns yield 45% higher engagement than manual outreach. Track open rates weekly—delete lists with <20% opens.

### 4. Community Partnerships: Get Free Promotion
Spend $50/month on cross-promotions:
- Buy 50 “Free Muffin” cards for a nearby bookstore
- Host “Yoga + Coffee” mornings with a local studio
- Offer 10% off to students with university ID

A Seattle coffee shop boosted sales 28% by teaming up with a dog groomer—pet owners started visiting twice weekly. Track partnerships with unique promo codes (use bit.ly links).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I track if Google Ads are working?**  
Use your GBP’s “Insights” tab to see ad-driven traffic. If you’re getting 20+ clicks/month with a <4% cost-per-conversion, keep the campaign.

**Can I skip SEO and just do ads?**  
No. Ads get you new customers, but SEO keeps them coming back. 60% of GBP searches happen on a 0.5-mile radius—opt in!

**What if my city has big chains like Starbucks?**  
Focus on “hidden gem” keywords: “best [Your City] coffee shop for remote work” or “quiet coffee near [local park].”

**Should I spend on Instagram Stories?**  
Only if your customers are under 30. For older demographics, Facebook Events and Google Posts drive 3x more foot traffic.

**How long before I see results?**  
SEO takes 3–6 months, ads show results in 2–3 weeks. Automate everything and measure weekly.
