### Social Media Hacks That Don’t Require a Marketing Degree  

You don’t need a full-time social media manager. Just 2 hours/week can make a difference.  

**1. Post 3 times per week consistently**  
- Monday: “Weekend latte art special” with a photo of your barista’s best work  
- Wednesday: “Tag us in your coffee pic and we’ll surprise you with a free pastry!”  
- Friday: “Last chance to join our #CoffeeClub and get 10% off your next purchase”  

**2. Use free tools like Canva and Unsplash**  
Design eye-catching graphics for $0. Canva’s “Coffee Shop” template library has 100+ free designs.  

**3. Leverage user-generated content**  
When a customer tags your shop, reshare their post with a thank-you comment. This costs *nothing* but makes your page feel vibrant.  

**Case Study**: A Brooklyn café boosted Instagram engagement by 60% by running a “Best Caffe Mocha Shot” contest. Winners got a free espresso, and the contest generated 250+ user posts.

### Google Business Profile: The $150 That Drives 70% of Your Traffic  

Your Google Business Profile is your shop’s digital storefront. Here’s how to optimize it for $150/month:  

1. **Claim your profile** and add high-res photos (interior, baristas, daily specials)  
2. **Post weekly updates** like “New cold brew blend available today!”  
3. **Collect 10+ reviews/month** by training your staff to ask: “Would you like to leave a review for us?”  

**Actionable Strategy**: Use Google’s free “Smart Campaigns” tool to create location-based ads. For $150/month, you can target people within 0.5 miles of your shop and show ads like:  

> “Stop by The Daily Grind for your morning coffee! 10% off with this code: GRIND10”  

**Result**: One Austin café increased Google Ads CTR by 40% using this approach.

### Email Marketing That Doesn’t Suck  

You’re not sending another generic “Happy Friday!” blast. Let’s get *specific*.  

1. **Create a lead magnet**  
   - Offer a free “How to Brew the Perfect Pour-Over” guide in exchange for emails  
   - Use a free tool like Mailchimp or Sendinblue (both offer free tiers!)  

2. **Send 2 emails/week** with value:  
   - “Your barista’s favorite seasonal drink recipe”  
   - “Your birthday is coming up – claim your free pastry!”  

3. **Automate rewards**  
   Set up an email autoresponder for customers who refer a friend. Example:  
   > “Thanks for referring Sam! You both get 15% off next visit.”  

**Real Stat**: Coffee shops with email lists see **25% higher customer retention** than those without.

### Partnerships That Cost Less Than a Muffin  

Your $15/month budget can unlock major local love:  

1. **Collaborate with nearby bookstores**  
   Cross-promote with a deal like: “Show your bookstore loyalty card for 10% off coffee.”  

2. **Sponsor a local event**  
   Offer $25 worth of coffee samples at a neighborhood farmer’s market in exchange for social media mentions.  

3. **Partner with freelancers**  
   Offer free coffee to remote workers in exchange for them sharing your “Remote Work Happy Hour” post.  

**Example**: A Denver café partnered with a co-working space and saw a 15% spike in afternoon traffic.
