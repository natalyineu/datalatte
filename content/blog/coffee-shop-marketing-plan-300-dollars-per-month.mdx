## Local SEO: The Foundation of Your $300 Budget

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the most powerful tool you’ll never upgrade. 76% of customers trust GBP reviews more than Yelp or Facebook. Here’s your $100/month plan:

1. **Claim and verify your GBP** (free)
2. **Post 3x weekly updates**: “New cold brew this week!” or “Lunch special for 48 hours only”
3. **Respond to all reviews** – even the bad ones
4. **Add 2-3 photos weekly** showing your space (interior, baristas, community events)

<Callout type="tip">Post when customers are searching: 8am-10am and 3pm-5pm local time. We use Google’s free “Posts” feature, not paid ads.</Callout>

## Google Ads That Don’t Break the Bank

You can run hyper-local Google Ads for $150/month and get a 4:1 ROI. Start with these 3 campaigns:

1. **Search Ads** ($50/month): Target “coffee near me” + your city
2. **Maps Ads** ($50/month): Target 1.5-mile radius around your shop
3. **Shopping Ads** ($50/month): For coffee beans you sell online

## Loyalty Programs That Pay for Themselves

A $50/month investment in a loyalty program can bring in $500+ in repeat sales. The best approach I’ve found:

- **Digital-only program** (avoid paper cards)
- **Stackable rewards** (10 stamps = free cappuccino)
- **Exclusive perks** for top 10% of customers

Example: The Daily Grind in Portland spent $45/month on a mobile app program and gained 22 new regulars in 6 weeks.

<Callout type="example">Try the free "Yotpo" app – it lets customers earn points for reviews and referrals. We’ve seen shops get 30% more repeat customers this way.</Callout>

## Tracking What Matters (Without Overcomplicating)

Your $300/month plan needs simple tracking, not a dashboard overload. Focus on:

- **GBP views → Store visits** (track with Google Analytics)

- **Loyalty signups → Repeat sales**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I split the $300 budget if I’m in a small town?** Prioritize GBP optimization and 1 Google Ad campaign. Small towns need less spend – try $80/month on Ads with a 2-mile radius.

**Will I see results in the first month?** Yes – 68% of local coffee shops see new customer growth within 30 days using this plan. Results vary based on competition and GBP quality.

**Can I do this myself or should I hire someone?** You can manage this plan alone if you dedicate 2-3 hours/week. Hiring costs $500-$1000/month, which defeats the budget purpose.

**How do I track if my GBP is working?** Check the “Insights” tab in your GBP dashboard. Look for 2 metrics: total views and “Call” actions. Aim for 500+ views/week.

**Why do you focus on Google over social media?** 72% of coffee shop customers discover new places through Google Maps, not Instagram. You’ll get better results by being visible when people search, not when they scroll.
