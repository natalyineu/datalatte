## Fix Your Google Business Profile Before Spending a Dime  

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is your #1 lead magnet—if it’s not optimized, you’re losing 60% of potential customers before they walk in. Most cafes waste time editing bios or adding 20 generic photos. Here’s what actually works:  

- **Claim GBP** if you haven’t already (it’s free).  
- **Post 5–7 high-res photos** of your space, baristas, and signature drinks. Use a smartphone on a tripod—no filters.  
- **Set up Google Posts** for flash sales: “$2 off cold brews all day Friday” drives 3x more clicks than vague “We’re open!” updates.  
- **Add 3 unique attributes** like “Outdoor Seating” or “Latte Art” to show up in filtered searches.  

<Callout type="tip">Include your most popular drink in the GBP “About” section. Example: “Award-winning cold brew made with beans from [Local Roaster].”</Callout>  

## Run Facebook Ads That Cost Less Than Your Barista’s Tip Jar  

$100/month on Meta Ads isn’t a waste—if you follow these rules. Most coffee shops waste money on vague audiences like “Coffee Lovers” (they’re not real). Instead:  

1. **Create a Custom Audience** from your email list or past app downloads (ask customers to opt in).  
2. **Use Facebook Lead Ads** for free coffee samples in exchange for emails. Offer 100 leads for $20–$30.  
3. **Run Location-Based Retargeting**: Show ads to people who visited your store but didn’t sign up for your loyalty program.

## Local SEO Tactics That Cost Less Than $100/Month  

You don’t need an agency to rank for “coffee near me.” For $50/month, do this:  

- **Claim 3–5 local directories**: Yelp, Foursquare, CitySearch. Fill out 100% of fields.  
- **Get 10 quality reviews/month**: Ask customers to snap a photo of their drink and post it online—offer a free pastry in return.  
- **Write 1 blog post/month**: “5 Reasons Why [Your Café Name] is Austin’s Best Spot for Remote Work.” Link to your menu.  

<Callout type="coffee">Example: “Daily Grind Café” in Portland saw 40% more walk-ins after adding “Pet-Friendly Seating Area” to their GBP and optimizing for “coffee near me with WiFi.”</Callout>  

## Automate 80% of Your Marketing With Zero Coding  

Marketing automation isn’t just for $5,000/month brands. For $50/month, use tools like Mailchimp or ActiveCampaign to:  

- **Send a “Welcome Back” email** to customers who haven’t visited in 21 days (attach a 20% off coupon).  
- **Automate loyalty rewards**: “Buy 5 coffees, get 1 free” tracked via a free app like Loyverse.  
- **Trigger SMS alerts** when someone searches “open coffee shops near me” within 0.5 miles of your location.  

## Frequently Asked Questions  

**How much should I spend on Google Ads for my coffee shop?**  
Start with $150/month for text ads targeting “coffee near me [Your City].” If cost per click exceeds $2.50, pause and refine your keywords.  

**Is local SEO worth it for coffee shops?**  
Yes. 76% of people who search “coffee near me” visit a store within 5 miles. Focus on GBP optimization and 10+ quality reviews.  

**Can I use Instagram for coffee shop marketing?**  
Only if you have time to post 3x/week visually. Otherwise, focus on Facebook Ads—it has better targeting for local businesses.  

**What’s the best loyalty program for a small café?**  
A simple punch card (“Buy 9 coffees, get 1 free”) works best. Digital tools add friction for cash-paying customers.  

**Why isn’t my Google Business Profile showing up first?**  
Add 100% complete hours, include 3+ categories (e.g., “Café,” “Coffee Shop,” “Bakery”), and respond to every review.
