### 1. Start With Your Google Business Profile (It’s Free)
Your Google Business Profile is the first thing customers see. 80% of users trust reviews as much as personal recommendations. Fix these three things immediately:

1. **Claim your profile** (if you haven’t already).
2. **Add 5–7 high-res photos** of your café, baristas, and drinks.
3. **Post weekly Google Posts** about your signature latte or morning specials.

<Callout type="tip">Use “Open Now” in your Google Posts. I’ve seen this boost walk-ins by 25% for downtown Seattle coffee shops.</Callout>

### 2. Spend $150/Month on Targeted Google Ads
Google Ads gets a bad rap, but done right, it works. Allocate $150/month to:

- **Location-based keywords**: “coffee near me,” “best espresso [City Name]”
- **Mobile-only bids**: 60% of coffee shop searches happen on phones
- **Peak hours targeting**: 7–9 AM and 3–5 PM

### 3. Build a $100/Month Local SEO Flywheel
SEO isn’t instant, but these 3 steps show results in 8–12 weeks:

1. **Fix your NAP (Name, Address, Phone)**: 45% of coffee shops have inconsistent listings.
2. **Get 10+ reviews/month**: Ask customers to review you after their first visit.
3. **Create 3 content pillars**:
   - Your story (e.g., “Why we roast our own beans”)
   - Local guides (“Top 5 coffee spots in [Neighborhood]”)
   - Seasonal posts (“What to order during fall at [Your Shop]”)

<Callout type="coffee">I recommend writing one 500-word blog post per month and turning it into 3 social posts. A Denver café boosted organic traffic by 3x with this method.</Callout>

### 4. Automate $50/Month Loyalty Marketing
You don’t need expensive software. Use free tools like:

- **Canva** for designing “Buy 5 coffees, get 1 free” punch cards
- **Google Forms** to collect emails for a “30% off your next order” list
- **Zapier** to auto-post to Facebook when you post on Instagram

<Callout type="example">A Boston bakery automated email reminders for its “Tuesday Todd” promotion. Result: 20% more repeat customers.</Callout>

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How much should I spend on Google Ads?**  
Start with $150/month. If your cost per customer is under $8, you can increase the budget. If not, pause and optimize.

**Can I do SEO without hiring someone?**  
Yes. Focus on 3-5 keywords like “coffee near [City Name]” and update your Google Business Profile weekly.

**What if I don’t have time to post daily?**  
Schedule 2 Google Posts/week and 1 Facebook post. Your customers won’t notice the difference.

**Should I care about 1-star reviews?**  
Absolutely. One bad review can drop your conversion rate by 30%. Respond instantly and fix the issue privately.

**How long until I see results?**  
Local SEO takes 3–6 months. Paid ads show results in 2–3 weeks if targeted correctly.

**Is marketing automation worth it?**  
Yes, but start small. Automate email birthday greetings or loyalty reminders first.

**What if my website is ugly?**  
Fix it in 2 hours: Use a free Squarespace template, add your menu, and include “Open Now” from Google Business Profile.
