## What to Spend Your $300 On Each Month  

1. **$150 on Google Ads** for 5-mile radius targeting  
2. **$75 on local SEO** (Google Business Profile + Yelp)  
3. **$50 on a loyalty SMS tool** like Attentive or Moey  
4. **$25 on monthly social posts** (Instagram + Facebook)  

<Callout type="tip">Start with Google Ads first. 72% of local searches end in a store visit within 24 hours.</Callout>  

For example, *The Daily Grind* in Austin, TX spent $150/month on Google Ads with a 5-mile radius. They got 12 new customers daily—enough to cover costs in 3 weeks.  

## Why Google Ads Works for Coffee Shops  

Local Google Ads cost $1.50–$2.00 per click for coffee shops in 2026. With a $150/month budget, you’ll get 75–100 clicks. Half of those will be people within 2 miles of your shop.

<Callout type="warning">Don’t use broad keywords like “best coffee.” Use “coffee shop near me” with location + time (e.g., “Austin coffee shop open at 6am”).</Callout>  

Set your Google Ads to run 7am–10am and 3pm–6pm. That’s when 68% of coffee shops see 70% of their walk-ins.  

## Local SEO: The $75 That Gets You Found First  

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is either a 5-star review magnet or a blank page with 3 photos of the same latte. Fix that with:  

- **5–7 high-res photos** (interior, menu, your team)  
- **3 fresh posts/month** (e.g., “New seasonal drink: Maple Pecan Latte”)  
- **10 verified reviews/month** (Ask customers to review via email or SMS)  

<Callout type="example">*Brewed Awakenings* in Seattle added 4 new GBP posts and 15 reviews in 2 weeks. Their GBP visibility score rose from 64% to 89% in 30 days.</Callout>  

Use Ahrefs’ Site Crawler ($75/month) to find local keywords. Search “Austin coffee shop + [your city]” to steal keywords from competitors.  

## Automate Your Loyalty Program for Free  

You think your customers are loyal? Only 22% of coffee shop patrons visit more than once per week. Fix this with a **$50/month SMS automation** plan.  

Steps to build loyalty:  
1. Offer a **10-cup discount** via email/SMS (30% off)  
2. Send a **“Your cup is 1 away from free”** message on birthdays  
3. A/B test **“Buy 5 coffees, get 1 free”** vs “$50 off 10 drinks”  

<Callout type="coffee">DataLatte uses Moey for our clients—it integrates with Square and automates 80% of loyalty tasks. Try their $25/month Basic plan.</Callout>  

This costs $50/month but cuts customer churn by 40%+ for small cafes.  

## The $25 Social Media Hack You Can Do in 20 Minutes  

Your Instagram is 20 posts of coffee beans. Stop. Post:  
- 2x customer photos per week (#MyDailyGrind)  
- 1x behind-the-scenes story daily (barista of the day)  
- 3x time-sensitive offers (e.g., “$2 off iced coffee after 3pm”)

## Frequently Asked Questions  

**How do I track if this works?**  
Check Google Analytics for “new users” daily. If you get 50+ new visitors/week, keep spending $300/month.  

**Can I do this without a website?**  
No. Get a free website via Shopify or WordPress. A $20/month domain name is worth the cost.  

**What if I’m too busy?**  
Outsource the Google Ads to a micro-agency for $200/month. Keep the SEO and SMS in-house.  

**Will this work for a new coffee shop?**  
Yes—but spend 50% of your budget on Yelp Ads first. 63% of first-time visitors come from Yelp.  

**How long until results?**  
3–4 weeks for Google Ads, 2 months for local SEO. Start with Ads first.
