## Google Ads: Spend $200/month to Target Coffee Lovers Within 3 Miles

Google Ads is your fastest way to show up when locals search for “best coffee near me.” Start with a **geo-targeted search campaign** using keywords like “artisan coffee [Your City]” and “cold brew [Your Neighborhood].” 

Here’s how to make it work:
- **Set a daily budget of $6.50** to spend $200/month total
- Use **location extensions** to show your address in ads
- Create 2-3 ad variations testing “Free pastry with first visit” vs “10% off large orders”

Austin’s *Brew Haven* spent $200/month on this strategy and saw **28 new weekly customers** in 60 days. Their best-performing keyword? “Coffee with vegan pastries.”

## Local SEO: Get Free Traffic with Zero Ongoing Cost

Ranking for “coffee shop near me” is free but takes work. Your goal: **dominate the first page of Google Maps results**.

Follow this checklist:
1. **Claim your Google Business Profile** and add 15+ photos (including your baristas)
2. Get **10+ reviews/month** by asking customers to scan a QR code after purchase
3. Update your **Google Posts** weekly with events like “$2 off lattes on Wednesdays”

Portland’s *Bean & Bloom* increased foot traffic by 32% after adding “seasonal drink specials” to their Google Posts. Use tools like **BrightLocal** for $50/month to track your rankings.

## Social Media: Post 3x/Week to Build Community

You don’t need a social media manager. Post **3 times/week on Instagram and Facebook** using a free scheduler like **Buffer**. Focus on:
- **Behind-the-scenes content** (e.g., “How we roast our beans”)
- **User-generated content** (regram customer photos with permission)
- **Daily specials** with clear timers (e.g., “$1 off iced coffee until 2pm”)

Seattle’s *Rainy Day Roasters* grew their Instagram following by 180% using this mix. Their best-performing posts? **Photos of their baristas with captions like “Ask us about our secret menu!”**

<Callout type="warning">Don’t waste time on TikTok unless you’re targeting Gen Z with viral trends. Most local coffee shop owners see better results on Instagram Stories.</Callout>

## Loyalty Program: Turn First-Timers into Weekly Visitors

A $50/month loyalty program can boost repeat sales more than any ad. Use **Yotpo Points** or **Grocery Loyalty** to create:
- **Earn 10 points per $1 spent**
- **Redeem 100 points for a free pastry**
- **Birthday rewards** with a free drink

Toronto’s *Morning Grind* saw **40% more repeat customers** after implementing this system. Combine with a referral bonus: “Tell a friend, get $5 off your next order.”

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I know if my Google Ads are working?**  
Track your cost per lead (CPL). For coffee shops, aim for **$20 CPL or lower**. If you’re paying more, pause underperforming keywords like “coffee delivery” and focus on “coffee shop near me.”

**Can I skip social media and save money?**  
Only if you run a loyalty program *and* perfect your Google Business Profile. But local coffee shops with active Instagram accounts get **23% more walk-ins** than those without.

**Should I spend on Facebook Ads or Google Ads?**  
Start with Google Ads. Coffee shop owners see better results from **search ads** than Facebook’s vague “interest-based” targeting.

**How do I get more Google reviews?**  
Print QR codes linking to your Google Reviews page and place them at checkout. Offer a free cookie to customers who leave a review (within Google’s guidelines).

**Is $300/month enough for marketing?**  
Yes, if you focus on **Google Ads ($200), local SEO ($50), and social media ($50)**. Anything more is wasted on unnecessary tools or agency fees.
