### Tip: Start Small

Don't try to automate everything at once. Start with a small automation and test it thoroughly before scaling up. This will help you identify potential issues and ensure that your automations run smoothly.

### Warning: Be Careful with Triggers

Triggers are the events that trigger your automation. Be careful not to set up too many triggers, as this can lead to unnecessary messages and overwhelm your customers. Use triggers strategically and test them regularly to ensure they're working as intended.
