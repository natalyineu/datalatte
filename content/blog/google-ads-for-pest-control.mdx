## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Google Ads?
A: Google Ads is a paid advertising platform that allows businesses to create text-based ads that appear on Google's search engine results pages (SERPs).

Q: How do I set up a Google Ads account?
A: To set up a Google Ads account, you'll need to create a Google account and follow the setup wizard. You'll need to provide information about your business, including your name, address, and phone number.

Q: What is a Google Ads campaign?
A: A Google Ads campaign is a group of ads that share a common goal, such as driving website traffic or generating leads.

Q: How do I track conversions in Google Ads?
A: To track conversions in Google Ads, you'll need to set up conversion tracking in your Google Ads account. This involves adding code to your website to track conversions and linking them to your Google Ads campaigns.

Q: Can I use Google Ads for local SEO?
A: Yes, you can use Google Ads to improve your local SEO. By targeting specific locations and keywords, you can increase your visibility in local search results.

Q: How much does Google Ads cost?
A: The cost of Google Ads depends on your budget and bidding strategy. You'll need to set a daily budget and bid on keywords to determine how much you'll pay for each ad click.

Q: Can I use Google Ads with other advertising platforms?
A: Yes, you can use Google Ads with other advertising platforms, such as Facebook Ads and Instagram Ads. By using multiple platforms, you can reach a wider audience and increase your marketing effectiveness.
