### 3. Optimize Your Business Description

### 4. Use High-Quality and Relevant Images

Glovo uses images to understand the relevance of your business to specific searches. Use high-quality and relevant images that showcase your business's products or services. This will help Glovo understand your business's offerings and increase your visibility in search results.
