**Choosing the Right Ad Format**

Wolt offers various ad formats, including sponsored listings, banner ads, and even video ads. But which one is right for your restaurant? Here's a breakdown of the most popular options:

- **Sponsored Listings**: These ads appear at the top of search results and can be highly effective for local restaurants. They're also relatively affordable, with a cost-per-click (CPC) of around $0.50-$1.50.
- **Banner Ads**: These ads are displayed on the Wolt homepage and can be a great way to reach a wider audience. However, they can be more expensive than sponsored listings, with a CPC of around $1-$3.
- **Video Ads**: These ads are a great way to showcase your restaurant's atmosphere and cuisine. They can be more expensive than other ad formats, but can also drive more sales.

**Optimizing Your Ad Strategy**

Once you've chosen the right ad format, it's time to optimize your strategy. Here are some tips to get you started:

- **Target Your Audience**: Make sure you're targeting the right audience with your ads. Use Wolt's targeting options to reach customers who are most likely to be interested in your restaurant.
- **Use High-Quality Imagery**: Use high-quality images and videos to showcase your restaurant's food and atmosphere.
- **Offer Promotions**: Offer promotions and discounts to incentivize customers to try your restaurant.
- **Monitor Your Results**: Keep a close eye on your ad results and adjust your strategy as needed.
