**Getting started with Ads Manager**

To get started with Ads Manager, you'll need to create a Facebook account (if you haven't already) and set up your business page. Once you've done this, you can access Ads Manager from your Facebook page.

Here are the steps to follow:
1. Go to your Facebook page and click on the "Create Ad" button.
2. Choose your ad objective (e.g., website traffic, conversions, etc.).
3. Set your budget and schedule.
4. Choose your targeting options (e.g., location, age, interests, etc.).
5. Upload your ad creative (e.g., image, video, etc.).

**Optimizing your ads for better performance**

To get the most out of your ads budget, you'll need to optimize your ads for better performance. Here are some tips to follow:
* **Target the right audience**: Use Facebook's targeting options to reach the right people with your ads.

* **Set clear ad objectives**: Set clear ad objectives to ensure your ads are driving real results.
