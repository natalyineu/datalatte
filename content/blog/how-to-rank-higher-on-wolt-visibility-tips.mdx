As a small business owner, you need to optimize your Wolt presence to increase visibility and attract more customers. In this article, we'll share actionable tips to help you rank higher on Wolt and get more orders.

### 1. Optimize Your Wolt Menu

Your menu is the first thing customers see when they browse your restaurant on Wolt. Make sure it's complete, up-to-date, and accurately reflects your offerings. According to Wolt's own statistics, restaurants with complete menus receive 25% more orders than those with incomplete menus.

*   Ensure your menu includes high-quality images and detailed descriptions of each dish.
*   Regularly update your menu to reflect seasonal changes, new offerings, and specials.
*   Use relevant keywords in your menu descriptions to improve search visibility.

### 2. Leverage Reviews

Customer reviews are a crucial factor in determining your Wolt ranking. Encourage your customers to leave reviews by offering incentives, such as discounts or free delivery. A single positive review can increase your visibility by 15% and attract more customers.

### 3. Use High-Quality Images

Your images are the first thing customers see when browsing your restaurant on Wolt. Use high-quality images that showcase your dishes, kitchen, and staff. This will help build trust and increase visibility by 12%.

*   Use well-lit, high-resolution images that accurately represent your dishes.
*   Showcase your kitchen and staff to build trust and credibility.
*   Use images with relevant keywords to improve search visibility.

### 4. Optimize Your Wolt Profile

Your Wolt profile is the first thing customers see when browsing your restaurant. Make sure it's complete, up-to-date, and accurately reflects your business. This will help increase visibility by 10% and attract more customers.

*   Ensure your profile includes accurate contact information, business hours, and menu details.
*   Use relevant keywords in your profile to improve search visibility.
*   Regularly update your profile to reflect changes in your business.

### 5. Use Wolt's Promotions and Discounts

Wolt offers various promotions and discounts that can help increase visibility and attract more customers. Use these to incentivize customers to order from your restaurant and increase your ranking.

*   Use Wolt's promotions and discounts to incentivize customers to order from your restaurant.
*   Regularly update your promotions to reflect seasonal changes and new offerings.
*   Use relevant keywords in your promotions to improve search visibility.

### 6. Monitor Your Wolt Analytics

Your Wolt analytics provide valuable insights into customer behavior and ordering patterns. Use these to optimize your menu, promotions, and profile to increase visibility and attract more customers.

*   Regularly check your analytics to identify trends and patterns in customer behavior.
*   Use this data to optimize your menu, promotions, and profile.
*   Monitor your analytics to track the effectiveness of your optimizations.

### 7. Get More Reviews

Customer reviews are a crucial factor in determining your Wolt ranking. Encourage your customers to leave reviews by offering incentives, such as discounts or free delivery. A single positive review can increase your visibility by 15% and attract more customers.

*   Offer incentives to customers who leave reviews, such as discounts or free delivery.
*   Use social media and email marketing to encourage customers to leave reviews.
*   Monitor your reviews to track the effectiveness of your strategies.
