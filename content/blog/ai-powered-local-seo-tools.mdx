**1. Google My Business Optimization**

Google My Business (GMB) is a crucial tool for small businesses looking to improve their local SEO. However, optimizing your GMB listing can be time-consuming and requires a deep understanding of the algorithm.

Here are some key steps to optimize your GMB listing:

* Claim and verify your business listing
* Complete and accurate business information
* Add high-quality photos and videos
* Respond promptly to customer reviews

By following these steps, you can improve your GMB listing's visibility and drive more customers to your doorstep.

**2. Local SEO Audits**

A local SEO audit can help you identify areas for improvement and provide actionable tips to boost your online presence. Here's a breakdown of what to expect from a local SEO audit:

* Website audit: identifies technical issues and optimization opportunities
* GMB audit: evaluates listing accuracy and completeness
* Local citation audit: checks online directories and review sites
* SEO competitor analysis: identifies gaps in the market and opportunities for differentiation

By investing in a local SEO audit, you can gain a deeper understanding of your online presence and make data-driven decisions to drive more customers to your doorstep.
