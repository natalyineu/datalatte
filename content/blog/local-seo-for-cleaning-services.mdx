### Set Up Your Google Business Profile First  
Your GBP listing is the foundation of local SEO. Start by claiming your profile and filling out every field:  
- **Business name**: Include your city (e.g., “BrightSpot Cleaning - Austin”).  
- **Categories**: Choose “Cleaning Services” and a subcategory like “Residential Cleaning”.  
- **Photos**: Upload 10+ high-quality images of your work, team, and office.  
- **Posts**: Share weekly updates like “30% off summer deep cleans” to boost visibility.  

<Callout type="tip">Add 100+ characters to your “About” section with keywords like “house cleaning in [City]” and “affordable maid services [Neighborhood]”.</Callout>  

Get your GBP link: [Google Business Profile optimization](/services/google-business-profile).

### Optimize Your Website for Local Keywords  
Your website needs 3–5 high-intent local keywords. For a Houston-based cleaner, these might be:  
- “pressure washing Houston”  
- “move out cleaning services”  
- “affordable cleaning in [Neighborhood]”  

Use these keywords in:  
- H1 tags on service pages  
- Meta descriptions (keep them under 155 characters)  
- Image alt text (e.g., “pressure washing services in Houston, TX”)

### Collect 5+ Reviews Per Month  
Review quantity and quality matter more than you think.  
**How to get reviews:**  
1. Ask clients to leave a 5-star review via text after service.  
2. Offer a $5 credit for submitting a detailed review.  
3. Respond to every review—even negative ones.  

<Callout type="warning">Don’t let 1–2 bad reviews hurt your reputation. Fix the issue first, then ask the client to update their review.</Callout>  

Businesses with 100+ reviews see a 30–50% increase in GBP visibility.

### Build Local Citations Smartly  
List your business on 20+ local directories like:  
- Yelp (use same name, address, phone as GBP)  
- Angie’s List for service providers  
- City-specific sites like “Austin Home Services”  

<Callout type="coffee">Consistency is king. One typo in your address across 10 sites will drop your rankings by 40%+.</Callout>  

### Fix These 3 Common Local SEO Mistakes  
1. **Using generic keywords**: “cleaning services” without your city.  
2. **Ignoring GBP posts**: Monthly updates boost GBP visibility by 22%.  
3. **Not claiming all names**: If clients search for “Mike’s Cleaning Co”, make sure your GBP uses the same name.
