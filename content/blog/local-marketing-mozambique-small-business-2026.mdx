Mozambique is a Southeast African country of 33 million people with a 2,700km Indian Ocean coastline — one of the world's longest. Maputo is the capital and most developed city, a vibrant port city with Art Deco architecture and excellent food (piri-piri prawns, matapa). Beira is the second city; Nampula is the northern commercial hub; Pemba is the northern coastal gateway.

Mozambique is a Portuguese-speaking African nation (like Angola and Cape Verde) with a large Swahili-speaking population in the north. M-Pesa (Vodacom Mozambique) is the dominant mobile money platform, transforming commerce as dramatically as in Kenya. Facebook dominates social media with WhatsApp as the universal business tool.

## Mozambique's Digital Platform Landscape

| Platform | Active Users (Mozambique) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | 5.5M (17%) | Dominant social platform |
| WhatsApp | 7M (21%) | Primary business messaging |
| YouTube | 5M (15%) | Growing consumption |
| TikTok | 4M (12%) | Fast growing youth |
| Instagram | 2M (6%) | Urban youth |
| Google | 90%+ search share | Dominant |

**WhatsApp + Facebook core duo**: Mozambican business runs on WhatsApp and Facebook. WhatsApp for direct customer communication, orders, and M-Pesa payment coordination. Facebook for business pages, community groups, and advertising.

**M-Pesa dominant payment**: Vodacom M-Pesa is Mozambique's primary mobile payment system — used by millions for everyday transactions. Accepting M-Pesa is non-negotiable for any consumer business. e-Mola (Emtel) and mKesh are secondary platforms.

**Portuguese + local languages**: Portuguese is the official language and medium of formal business. Ronga and Changana are spoken in the Maputo region; Makua in the north. Urban digital marketing in Maputo is in Portuguese.

Mozambican CPCs are among Africa's lowest. Competition for Google Ads is minimal — most businesses operate purely through Facebook and WhatsApp organically.

## Three Mozambican Business Examples

### 💇 Salão de Beleza, Maputo Sommerschield

**Strategy**: Facebook business page with beauty content in Portuguese, WhatsApp Business for appointments (M-Pesa for payments), Instagram for transformation photos, Google Maps listing.

**Budget**: MZN 15,000/month (≈$234): MZN 6,500 Facebook Ads, MZN 5,500 Google Ads, MZN 3,000 content.

### 🍽️ Restaurante, Polana Maputo

**Strategy**: Facebook for Maputo foodie groups, Instagram food photography (piri-piri, seafood), WhatsApp for reservations, Google Maps, M-Pesa for payments.

**Budget**: MZN 12,000/month (≈$188): MZN 5,000 Facebook Ads, MZN 4,500 Google Ads, MZN 2,500 content.

### 🏋️ Ginásio, Maputo

**Strategy**: Facebook fitness content, Instagram, Google Maps, WhatsApp for memberships, M-Pesa for monthly fees.

**Budget**: MZN 10,000/month (≈$156): MZN 4,200 Meta Ads, MZN 3,800 Google Ads, MZN 2,000 content.

**Mozambican Women's Day (April 7)**: Mozambique has its own Women's Day on April 7, celebrating the role of women in the liberation struggle. This is a significant commercial day for beauty, florists, and restaurants — different from International Women's Day on March 8.

**Independence Day (June 25)**: Mozambique's independence from Portugal in 1975. Major national celebration with commercial activity.
