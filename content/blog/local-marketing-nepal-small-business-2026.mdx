Nepal is a Himalayan kingdom of 30 million people nestled between India and China — home to eight of the world's ten highest mountains including Everest, ancient Hindu and Buddhist temples, and Kathmandu Valley's extraordinary cultural heritage. Kathmandu is the capital and dominant commercial city; Pokhara is the adventure tourism hub; Biratnagar is the largest city in the eastern plains (Terai).

Nepal's digital ecosystem is developing rapidly — smartphone adoption has accelerated, Facebook dominates, and TikTok has become extraordinarily popular with Nepali youth. Tourism is a pillar of Nepal's economy, creating a dual market: local Nepali consumers and international trekkers and tourists (particularly in Kathmandu and Pokhara).

## Nepal's Digital Platform Landscape

| Platform | Active Users (Nepal) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | 14M (47%) | Dominant social platform |
| YouTube | 12M (40%) | Very high consumption |
| TikTok | 10M (33%) | Explosive growth — top platform for youth |
| WhatsApp | 8M (27%) | Business messaging |
| Instagram | 4M (13%) | Growing urban youth |
| Viber | 3.5M (12%) | Declining but still used |
| Google | 95%+ search share | Dominant |

**TikTok is Nepal's youth phenomenon**: Nepal has one of South Asia's highest TikTok adoption rates — short-form video in Nepali (and sometimes Maithili or Newari) reaches millions of young Nepalis. For businesses targeting 18-35 year olds, TikTok organic content is extremely high-reach.

**Facebook cross-demographic**: Facebook remains the platform for community, news, and business discovery across all age groups. Facebook Marketplace is heavily used for commerce. Local Facebook groups are essential for community businesses.

**Tourism overlay**: Kathmandu and Pokhara have a large international visitor population — trekkers, volunteers, digital nomads. Tourism-facing businesses need English-language digital presence.

Nepali CPCs are among South Asia's lowest. Google Ads competition is minimal outside basic categories. Tourism keywords in English get international searches with moderate competition from established operators.

Pokhara — Lakeside (tourist), Newroad (local commercial).

## Three Nepali Business Examples

### ☕ Café, Thamel Kathmandu (Tourist + Local)

**Strategy**: Bilingual English/Nepali Instagram (international tourists + Kathmandu youth), Facebook for Kathmandu community, Google Maps listed in English for tourist discovery, Google Search Ads in English ("best café Thamel"), WhatsApp for reservations.

**Budget**: NPR 25,000/month (≈$189): NPR 11,000 Google Ads, NPR 9,000 Meta Ads, NPR 5,000 content.

### 💇 Beauty Parlour, Kathmandu

**Strategy**: TikTok transformation videos in Nepali (highest reach for budget), Facebook for Kathmandu community groups, Google Maps, Google Search Ads in Nepali, WhatsApp for appointments.

**Budget**: NPR 18,000/month (≈$136): NPR 8,000 Google Ads, NPR 6,500 Meta/TikTok Ads, NPR 3,500 content.

### 🏔️ Trekking Agency, Kathmandu

**Strategy**: English-primary website with Google Ads targeting "Everest base camp trek", "Annapurna circuit", TripAdvisor listing, Google Maps, Instagram for trek photography, WhatsApp for international bookings.

**Budget**: NPR 30,000/month (≈$227): NPR 14,000 Google Ads, NPR 10,000 Instagram Ads, NPR 6,000 content.

## Nepali Marketing Calendar

| Period | Opportunity |
|---|---|
| October-November | Dashain (biggest Hindu festival — 15 days) |
| October-November | Tihar/Diwali (Festival of Lights, 5 days after Dashain) |
| November | Chhath Puja (major in Terai) |
| April | Nepali New Year (Baisakh 1 — mid-April) |
| April | Holi (spring colour festival) |
| August | Teej (women's festival — beauty industry peak) |
| May | Mother's Day (2nd Sunday May — English-speaking format) |

**Dashain (October)**: Nepal's most important national holiday — a 15-day Hindu celebration culminating in Vijaya Dashami. Families buy new clothes, receive blessings, and celebrate. Beauty salons are fully booked for makeup and hair. Retail and gifting peak. Start campaigns October 1.

**Teej (August)**: A women's festival where women fast, wear red, and visit temples. Beauty salons see significant demand for traditional makeup, mehendi (henna), and hair services in the week before Teej. Specific to Nepal and northern India.

**Nepali New Year (Baisakh 1, mid-April)**: Nepal uses the Bikram Sambat calendar — the New Year falls in mid-April. A major commercial reset — new clothes, restaurant outings, and celebrations.

## FAQ

**Nepali or English for marketing?**
Nepali (Devanagari script) for local consumers — Facebook content, local Google Ads, TikTok. English for tourism-facing businesses, trek agencies, and international services. Many Kathmandu businesses do bilingual content.

**How important is TikTok vs. Facebook in Nepal?**
TikTok now rivals Facebook for reach among under-35 Nepalis — it has been an extraordinary growth story. For maximum reach with youth: use both. TikTok for organic reach and entertainment, Facebook for community and paid ads with broader demographics.
