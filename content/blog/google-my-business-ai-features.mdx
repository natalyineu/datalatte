### Understanding the Basics of Google My Business

Google My Business is a free tool that helps local businesses manage their online presence across Google services like Search and Maps. By claiming and verifying your business, you can access valuable insights and tools to help you attract more customers.

### The Power of Google My Business Insights

Google My Business Insights is a powerful tool that provides you with actionable data to help you understand how your business is performing online. With Insights, you can gain valuable insights into:

* How many people are searching for your business
* How many people are visiting your website
* How many people are calling your business
* How many people are visiting your physical location

### Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I access Google My Business Insights?
A: To access Insights, sign in to your Google My Business account and click on the "Insights" tab.

Q: What types of businesses can use Google My Business?
A: Any business with a physical location can use Google My Business.

Q: How do I improve my Google My Business ranking?
A: Use the data from Insights to inform your marketing strategy and focus on providing excellent customer service.

Q: Can I use Google My Business for online ordering or delivery?
A: Yes, Google My Business allows you to add online ordering and delivery options to your profile.

Q: How do I manage my Google My Business reviews?
A: Respond promptly to both positive and negative reviews to show customers you value their feedback.
