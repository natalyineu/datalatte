### FAQ

**Q: How much does a retargeting campaign cost?**
A: The cost of a retargeting campaign depends on the ad platform, ad spend, and targeting options. On average, small businesses spend between $100-$500 per month on retargeting campaigns.

**Q: How long does a retargeting campaign take to set up?**
A: Setting up a retargeting campaign can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on the complexity of the campaign and the ad platform used.

**Q: Can I use retargeting ads for multiple businesses?**
A: Yes, you can use retargeting ads for multiple businesses, but make sure to set up separate campaigns for each business to avoid targeting the same customers multiple times.

**Q: How do I know if my retargeting campaign is working?**
A: You can track the success of your retargeting campaign by monitoring metrics such as conversion rates, click-through rates, and cost per acquisition.

**Q: Can I use retargeting ads for events or promotions?**
A: Yes, you can use retargeting ads for events or promotions to remind customers about upcoming events or special offers.

**Q: How often should I refresh my retargeting campaign?**
A: It's recommended to refresh your retargeting campaign every 3-6 months to keep your ads fresh and relevant to your target audience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do retargeting ads work for local businesses?  
Retargeting ads use cookies or user data to show targeted ads to people who visited your website but didn’t book an appointment or make a purchase. For example, if 97% of your site visitors leave without booking, these ads remind them of your services and encourage them to return.  

### Are retargeting ads cost-effective for small businesses?  
Yes, with an average ROI of 20%, retargeting campaigns often outperform other ads. They focus on users already familiar with your business, reducing wasted spend compared to broad campaigns.  

### How can I target customers who abandoned their cart?  
Use pixel tracking or UTM parameters to identify cart abandoners (40% average rate). Then, serve them personalized ads with discounts or reminders to complete their purchase.  

### How long should a retargeting campaign run?  
Most campaigns run 30–60 days, depending on user behavior. Analyze performance weekly and adjust targeting or creatives if engagement drops below 15% of monthly ad spend.
