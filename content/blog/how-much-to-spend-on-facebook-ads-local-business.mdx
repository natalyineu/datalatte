**Setting a Realistic Facebook Ads Budget for Local Businesses**

To get started with Facebook ads, you'll need to set a budget. But how much should you spend? The answer depends on your business goals, target audience, and competition. Here are some general guidelines to consider:

* For a coffee shop or salon, a budget of $500-$1,000 per month is a good starting point.
* For a pet groomer or fitness studio, a budget of $500-$2,000 per month might be more suitable.
* For a local business with a highly competitive market, a budget of $2,000-$5,000 per month might be necessary.

**The Importance of Targeting in Facebook Ads**

Targeting is key to getting the most out of your Facebook ads budget. Make sure to focus on:

* Location targeting: focus on your local area or specific zip codes.
* Interest targeting: target people who are interested in your products or services.
* Lookalike targeting: target people who are similar to your existing customers.

**Common Facebook Ads Mistakes to Avoid**

Don't fall into these common traps:

* **Over-optimizing for clicks**: prioritize conversions over clicks.
* **Not testing ad creative**: test different ad images, headlines, and copy.
* **Not monitoring ad performance**: track your ad spend, clicks, and conversions regularly.
