**Why Snapchat Ads for Gyms and Fitness Studios Are a Game-Changer**

Snapchat ads offer a unique opportunity for gyms and fitness studios to reach 18–34 year olds in a highly targeted and engaging way. With features like geofilters, lenses, and augmented reality experiences, Snapchat ads can help businesses stand out in a crowded market and drive local foot traffic.

**Getting Started with Snapchat Ads**

To get started with Snapchat ads, you'll need to create a Snapchat ad account and set up a geotargeted campaign. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. **Create a Snapchat ad account**: Sign up for a Snapchat ad account and link it to your business Facebook page.
2. **Set up a geotargeted campaign**: Use Snapchat's geotargeting feature to target users within a specific radius of your gym or fitness studio.
3. **Choose your ad creative**: Select a high-quality image or video ad that showcases your gym or fitness studio's unique selling proposition (USP).
4. **Set your budget and bidding strategy**: Set a daily budget and choose a bidding strategy that works for your business.
