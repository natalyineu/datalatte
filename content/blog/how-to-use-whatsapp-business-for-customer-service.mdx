## Set Up WhatsApp Business in 4 Steps  
Start by downloading the app and linking it to your regular phone number. Add a business profile with your address, hours, and a quick link to your website. For $0, you get features like **labels** (tag "Salon A" vs. "Salon B" clients) and **automated away messages**.  

A coffee shop in Austin uses labels to separate "regulars" from "first-timers." When a regular texts "Can I order 2 lattes?" the barista knows exactly what to prepare.  

<Callout type="example"> The "Pet Paws" groomer in Sydney set up an away message: "We’re closed today. Text us anytime—we reply within 2 hours." Bookings from WhatsApp jumped **30%** in 2 weeks.</Callout>

## Automate First Responses Without Feeling Robot  
Use the **Quick Replies** feature to save phrases like:  
- "Your 3 PM haircut is confirmed!"  
- "Our hours are 7 AM–7 PM daily."  
- "Download the booking app here [link]."  

A yoga studio in Toronto automates responses for "class times" but adds a personal note for cancellations: "We’re sad to see you go—let us know if you need help finding another class."  

<Callout type="tip"> **Mix automation and humanity**: Use 1–2 automated replies for FAQs, but always follow up with a voice message or photo for cancellations or urgent requests.</Callout>

## Turn Messages Into Bookings (No App Needed)  
Create a **click-to-book** flow. For example:  
1. Add a WhatsApp number to your Google Business listing.  
2. When a customer texts, reply with a link to your online calendar.  
3. Confirm the booking via WhatsApp.  

A barbershop in Melbourne saw **25% more bookings** after adding this flow. Their message: "I’ll send you a booking link. Can you confirm your 4 PM slot?"

## Use WhatsApp for Customer Service Without Burning Out  
Set clear boundaries. For example:  
- Only respond between 9 AM–6 PM.  
- Use the **Away Message** feature for holidays.  
- Label urgent issues (e.g., "Gym equipment broken") and route them to staff.  

A fitness studio in Chicago reduced support time by **40%** by creating a FAQ PDF and sharing it for common questions like "Do you offer refunds?"  

<Callout type="warning"> **Don’t reply to every message instantly**: Clients expect fast replies, but you don’t need to drop everything for a text at 9 PM.</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions  

**Can I use WhatsApp Business for free?**  
Yes! The basic version is free. Paid features like broadcast lists ($15/month) or ads ($100+/campaign) are optional.  

**How do I get customers to message me?**  
Add your WhatsApp number to your website, Google Business listing, and receipts. A coffee shop in Seattle added "Text us for same-day orders" on cups and saw **20% more texts**.  

**Is WhatsApp safe for credit card payments?**  
No. Use it for booking confirmations, then direct payments to Stripe, Square, or PayPal.  

**How do I handle customer complaints?**  
Apologize immediately, offer a solution, and follow up in 24 hours. A salon in Toronto fixed a bad haircut by sending a $20 voucher and a photo of the new look.  

**Can I track WhatsApp results?**  
Use UTM parameters in links (e.g., `whatsapp://send?text=Book%20here:https://yourwebsite.com/booking`). Track clicks in Google Analytics.
