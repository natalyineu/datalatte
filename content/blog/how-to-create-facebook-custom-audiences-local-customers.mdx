### Step 1: Set Up Your Facebook Pixel

To create a Custom Audience, you need to have a Facebook Pixel installed on your website. This tiny piece of code collects data on your website visitors and sends it to Facebook. Don't worry, it's easy to install and takes just a few minutes.

### Step 2: Choose Your Data Source

Facebook Custom Audiences can be created from three main data sources: 

* Website traffic
* App activity
* Customer data (email, phone number, etc.)

Choose the source that best fits your business needs. For example, if you have a coffee shop, you might want to target customers who have visited your website or app.

### Step 3: Define Your Audience

Now it's time to get specific. Who do you want to target? Use Facebook's advanced targeting options to select demographics, interests, behaviors, and more. You can even upload your own customer data to create a Custom Audience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create a Facebook Custom Audience for my local business?

To create a Facebook Custom Audience, you'll need to upload a list of your past customers' email addresses or phone numbers to Facebook. This list should be in a CSV or TXT file format, with one customer per row. You can upload this list to Facebook Ads Manager, where it will be matched to existing Facebook users.

### What is the average cost of running a Facebook ad campaign for a local business?

The average cost-per-click (CPC) for Facebook ads is $0.70 for local businesses, according to DataLatte. However, this cost can vary depending on your target audience, ad creative, and bidding strategy. To get started, you can set a daily budget of $5-10 and adjust as needed to optimize your ad performance.

### Can I use Facebook Custom Audiences to target customers who have visited my website?

Yes, you can use Facebook Custom Audiences to target customers who have visited your website. To do this, you'll need to install a Facebook pixel on your website, which will track visitor activity and allow you to create a custom audience based on this data. This can help you reach users who have shown interest in your business but haven't yet made a purchase.

### How effective are retargeting ads on Facebook for local businesses?

Retargeting ads on Facebook can be highly effective for local businesses, with the potential to increase conversion rates by up to 400%, according to HubSpot. By targeting users who have already interacted with your business, you can remind them of your products or services and encourage them to make a repeat purchase. This can be especially effective for local businesses, where repeat customers are a key driver of revenue.

### What percentage of customers are more likely to shop at local businesses?

According to American Express, 71% of consumers prefer to shop at local businesses. This presents a significant opportunity for local business owners to attract and retain customers, and Facebook Custom Audiences can be a powerful tool in this effort. By targeting your past customers and encouraging them to make repeat purchases, you can take advantage of this preference and drive revenue for your business.
