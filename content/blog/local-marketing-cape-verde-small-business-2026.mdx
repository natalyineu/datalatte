Cape Verde (Cabo Verde) is an Atlantic island archipelago of 500,000 people — 10 volcanic islands 500km off the west coast of Africa, sitting between Africa, Europe, and the Americas. Praia on Santiago Island is the capital; Sal Island and Boa Vista are the flat, beach resort islands that form the tourism economy; São Vicente (Mindelo) is the cultural capital. Cape Verde uses the Cape Verdean Escudo (CVE), pegged to the Euro at 110 CVE = 1 EUR.

Cape Verde is a Portuguese-speaking African nation with strong ties to both Portugal and the diaspora (Cape Verdean communities in Boston, Providence, Rotterdam, and Lisbon). Tourism has become the dominant economic sector — primarily British, German, Portuguese, and Northern European beach holiday visitors to Sal and Boa Vista.

**Facebook dominant**: Cape Verde's social media centres on Facebook — community groups by island and neighbourhood are essential for local business discovery. Praia community groups are active.

**Instagram for tourism**: Cape Verde's white sand beaches and turquoise Atlantic waters are highly Instagrammable. Tourism businesses maintain English and German language Instagram accounts to reach European visitors.

**EUR-pegged economy**: CVE's fixed peg to EUR (110 CVE = 1 EUR) makes budget planning in European terms straightforward. Google Ads can be planned in EUR terms with simple conversion.

## Three Cape Verdean Business Examples

### 🏄 Kite Surfing School, Sal Island

**Strategy**: English and German Instagram showcasing Sal's world-famous kite surf conditions (Sal is one of the world's top kite surf destinations), Google Search Ads in English and German ("kite surfing lessons Sal Cape Verde", "Kitesurfen Kap Verde"), TripAdvisor listing, WhatsApp for European bookings.

**Budget**: CVE 120,000/month (≈€1,091): CVE 53,000 Google Ads, CVE 43,000 Meta Ads, CVE 24,000 content.

### 🍽️ Restaurant, Praia Santiago

**Strategy**: Facebook in Portuguese for Praia community, Instagram food photography, Google Maps, WhatsApp for reservations, MZN payments (local).

**Budget**: CVE 70,000/month (≈€636): CVE 30,000 Google Ads, CVE 27,000 Meta Ads, CVE 13,000 content.

### 🎵 Cultural Tour, São Vicente/Mindelo

**Strategy**: English and Portuguese Instagram showcasing Mindelo's Morna music culture (Cesária Évora's home city) and Carnival, Google Search Ads in English ("Cape Verde music culture tour", "Mindelo jazz festival"), TripAdvisor listing, WhatsApp for bookings.

**Budget**: CVE 90,000/month (≈€818): CVE 40,000 Google Ads, CVE 33,000 Meta Ads, CVE 17,000 content.

**Mindelense Carnival (February)**: São Vicente's Mindelo hosts one of the best carnivals in the Portuguese-speaking world — a smaller but supremely musical and creative Carnival. Beauty businesses see significant demand; tourism spikes on São Vicente.

**Peak tourism season (October-April)**: European beach holiday visitors — primarily British, German, and Scandinavian — arrive in largest numbers during European winter. Sal and Boa Vista businesses should maximise Google Ads from August through April. Kite surfing season aligns with consistent trade winds November-July.

**Independence Day (July 5)**: Cape Verde's independence from Portugal in 1975. National celebration with commercial activity.

## FAQ

**Portuguese or Cape Verdean Creole (Kriolu) for marketing?**
Portuguese for formal business and written marketing. Kriolu (Cabo Verdean Creole — unique to each island group, distinct from other creoles) in social media creates deep authenticity and warmth. English and German are essential for tourism-facing businesses.

**How important is the diaspora for Cape Verde marketing?**
Very significant. The Cape Verdean diaspora (in Boston/New England, Rotterdam, Lisbon) outnumbers the island population. Remittances are a major economic input. Some businesses target diaspora returning for visits or purchasing products to be sent home. Facebook targeting Cape Verdean diaspora communities can be commercially valuable.
