**Why Live Chat Matters for Local Businesses**

Live chat can make a significant difference in converting website visitors into paying customers. Here are some reasons why it matters:

*   **Instant Support**: Live chat provides instant support to customers, reducing the likelihood of them abandoning their carts or leaving your website without making a purchase.
*   **Personal Touch**: Live chat allows customers to interact with your business in real-time, creating a more personal and engaging experience.
*   **Increased Conversions**: Live chat has been shown to increase conversions by up to 20% (Source: HubSpot, 2022).

**Types of Live Chat Options**

There are several live chat options available for local businesses, including:

*   **Integrated Live Chat**: This type of live chat is integrated directly into your website, allowing customers to chat with your team in real-time.
*   **AI-Powered Live Chat**: This type of live chat uses artificial intelligence to provide instant support to customers, even when your team is not available.
*   **Third-Party Live Chat**: This type of live chat is provided by a third-party service, which can be integrated into your website.
