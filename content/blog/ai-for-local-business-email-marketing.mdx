**Step 1: Set Up Your Email List**

Before you can unlock AI-powered email marketing, you need a clean and active email list. This means:

* Collecting contact information from customers, website visitors, and social media followers
* Segmenting your list by demographics, behavior, and purchase history
* Regularly cleaning your list to remove inactive or unengaged subscribers
