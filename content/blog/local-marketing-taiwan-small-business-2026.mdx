Taiwan is East Asia's most underrated digital market. With 23 million people, 93% internet penetration, and one of Asia's most vibrant café and night market cultures, Taiwan offers a rich environment for local business digital marketing. Taipei is a cosmopolitan, food-obsessed capital. Taichung and Kaohsiung are major southern cities with distinct local characters.

Like Japan, Taiwan's digital ecosystem is shaped by LINE — the dominant messaging and business platform. Unlike Japan, Taiwan also has strong Facebook usage and a very active Instagram food and lifestyle community.

## Taiwan's Digital Platform Landscape

| Platform | Active Users (Taiwan) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| LINE | 21M (89% of pop.) | Dominant messaging AND marketing |
| YouTube | 18M (76%) | Very high consumption |
| Facebook | 15M (63%) | Strong, cross-demographic |
| Instagram | 9M (38%) | Lifestyle, food, beauty |
| TikTok | 6M (25%) | Growing with youth |
| Google | 60-65% search share | Important but Yahoo! holds share |
| Yahoo! Taiwan | ~30% search share | Significant — Yahoo! still relevant |

**LINE dominates just like Japan**: LINE has 89% penetration in Taiwan — every Taiwanese adult essentially uses LINE. LINE Official Accounts for businesses are essential — push messages, Rich Menus, and LINE Pay are all standard business tools.

**Yahoo! Taiwan still significant**: Unlike virtually every other market, Yahoo! Taiwan holds approximately 30% of search market share. Running ads on Yahoo! Taiwan (via Yahoo! Gemini or Taiwan-specific placements) reaches a meaningful audience beyond Google.

**Traditional characters**: Taiwanese content uses Traditional Chinese characters (繁體中文) — different from Simplified Chinese used in mainland China. All content must use Traditional Chinese.

## LINE Official Account for Taiwan

LINE OA is Taiwan's most important business marketing tool — identical in function to Japan's LINE OA:

1. Create at business.line.me (Taiwan portal)
2. Build Rich Menu with Traditional Chinese content
3. Set up broadcast messages in Traditional Chinese
4. Enable LINE Pay integration
5. Generate QR code for in-store display

Example broadcast (Traditional Chinese):
```
【優惠通知】✨
本週限定！剪髮+染髮套餐8折！
📅 6/14（六）～6/15（日）
🔗 立即預約：[連結]
限前15位，手刀預約！
```

## Taiwanese Food and Night Market Culture

Taiwan has one of the world's most extraordinary food cultures — night markets (夜市), beef noodles, bubble tea, scallion pancakes, and stinky tofu are global cultural exports. For food businesses:

- Google Maps optimisation is crucial (tourists heavily use Google Maps in Taiwan)
- Instagram food photography must be exceptionally high quality
- Bubble tea content has global virality potential
- Night market and street food content performs very well domestically

## Three Taiwanese Business Examples

### ☕ Specialty Coffee, Da'an District Taipei

**Strategy**: LINE OA for loyalty and promotions, Instagram with Taipei café aesthetic (very high quality expected), Yahoo! Taiwan + Google Search Ads, Facebook for Taipei community groups.

**Budget**: TWD 30,000/month (≈$938): TWD 12,000 Google/Yahoo Ads, TWD 10,000 Instagram Ads, TWD 8,000 LINE/content.

### 💇 髮廊 (Hair Salon), Taichung

**Strategy**: Instagram transformation content in Traditional Chinese, LINE OA for bookings and client retention, Google + Yahoo Search Ads for "髮廊台中", Google Maps.

**Budget**: TWD 20,000/month (≈$625): TWD 8,000 Google Ads, TWD 7,000 Instagram Ads, TWD 5,000 LINE/content.

### 🐾 寵物美容 (Pet Grooming), Taipei

**Strategy**: Instagram and TikTok before/after pet content in Traditional Chinese, LINE OA for booking, Google Search Ads, Google Maps.

**Budget**: TWD 15,000/month (≈$469): TWD 6,000 Google Ads, TWD 5,500 TikTok/Instagram Ads, TWD 3,500 LINE/content.

**Lunar New Year**: The biggest Taiwanese holiday — businesses close for 1-2 weeks. Pre-CNY beauty appointments spike dramatically. Start CNY marketing 3-4 weeks early.

**Father's Day August 8**: Taiwan celebrates Father's Day on August 8 — because "eight-eight" (八八, bā bā) sounds like "bàba" (爸爸, father) in Mandarin. Unique to Taiwan.

## FAQ

**Is LINE or Instagram more important for Taiwanese businesses?**
LINE for retention and direct communication (push messages get near-100% delivery to opted-in followers). Instagram for discovery and brand building. Both are essential — LINE drives repeat business, Instagram drives new customer acquisition.

**Should I use Traditional or Simplified Chinese?**
Traditional Chinese always for Taiwan. Using Simplified Chinese in Taiwan signals unfamiliarity with the market (Simplified is used in mainland China and Singapore). All marketing must be in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文).
