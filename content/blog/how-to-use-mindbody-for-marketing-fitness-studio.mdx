## Automating What Matters Most

You’re juggling class schedules, equipment maintenance, and payroll while trying to get new clients through the door. 71% of fitness studio owners say they *never* have enough time for marketing. Mindbody marketing automation isn’t just another tool — it’s your part-time marketing hire that works 24/7 to reduce no-shows, boost referrals, and keep your best clients coming back.

## Set Up Automated Welcome Campaigns

New clients who get a 3-email welcome series are 50% more likely to attend their second class. Here’s how to set it up in Mindbody:

1. Go to **Marketing > Automation** and create a new **Welcome Flow**
2. Add an instant email with your schedule, a 24-hour post-visit follow-up, and a 7-day loyalty nudge
3. Include a 10% discount code in the first email (codes cost me $0.50 each but boost retention by 30%)

A yoga studio in Austin, TX saw 28% more class sign-ups after adding this sequence. They spent 1 hour setting it up and saved 8 hours/week on manual outreach.

<Callout type="tip">**Use segmentation:** Create separate flows for yoga, personal training, and group cycling clients. Your barre clients don’t care about CrossFit class updates!</Callout>

## Automate Class Reminders That Convert

No-shows cost the average studio $1,200/month in lost revenue. Mindbody lets you:

- Send SMS reminders 24 hours before classes
- Add a “last-minute” email 1 hour before empty slots
- Set up auto-rescheduling for regulars

A 10-person Pilates studio in Toronto reduced no-shows from 22% to 9% by enabling these alerts. They kept the same $300/month budget but increased class attendance by 34%.

## Build a Referral Engine

Word-of-mouth drives 60% of new clients for small studios. Create a referral automation that:

1. Triggers after 3 class visits
2. Sends a personalized referral link
3. Auto-applies $25 credits when both parties sign up

This cost a dance studio in Chicago $120/month in credits but brought in 22 new clients. The automation runs entirely in Mindbody – no need for extra apps.

<Callout type="coffee">**My favorite hack:** Add a “Refer 2 friends, get a free class” prompt in your post-class survey. It’s low-pressure and super effective.</Callout>

## Track What Actually Works

Most studio owners check their Mindbody dashboard once a month – a mistake. Set up these weekly reports:

- **Automation performance:** Which flows have the highest open rates?
- **Client activity:** Which instructors keep clients coming back?
- **Marketing costs:** Is your referral program ROI positive?

One personal training studio found their 7-day re-engagement email had a 9% open rate vs 42% for their 3-day post-class follow-up. They shifted focus to the better-performing message.
