### Why Connected TV Ads for Local Businesses?

Connected TV ads offer a unique combination of reach, engagement, and measurability that's hard to find elsewhere. With platforms like Hulu, YouTube TV, and Peacock, you can target specific demographics, interests, and behaviors to reach the people most likely to be interested in your products or services.

But here's the thing: connected TV ads aren't just for big brands with big budgets. With programmatic advertising, you can set a budget and target specific audiences without breaking the bank.

### Setting Up Connected TV Ads for Your Local Business

So, how do you get started with connected TV ads? Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

1. **Identify your target audience**: Who are the people most likely to be interested in your products or services? Use data and research to pinpoint specific demographics, interests, and behaviors.
2. **Choose your platforms**: Select the connected TV platforms that align best with your target audience. For example, if you're targeting younger viewers, you might focus on YouTube TV or Hulu.
3. **Set your budget**: Determine how much you're willing to spend on connected TV ads and set a budget accordingly.
4. **Create your ad**: Use a combination of visual and audio elements to create an engaging ad that captures the attention of your target audience.
5. **Launch your campaign**: Use programmatic advertising to launch your connected TV ad campaign and start reaching your target audience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are connected TV ads and how do they work?

Connected TV ads are commercials that run on streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, or Disney+, and can be targeted to specific audiences based on demographics, interests, or behaviors. They work similarly to digital ads, but on a larger screen. With connected TV ads, you can reach an engaged audience of 150 million users in the US, projected to increase by 2026.

### How much does it cost to run a connected TV ad campaign?

The cost of a connected TV ad campaign varies depending on factors like ad format, targeting options, and frequency. However, studies have shown that connected TV ads can drive a 200% increase in ad recall compared to linear TV, making them a valuable investment for local businesses.

### Can connected TV ads really drive sales for my business?

Yes, connected TV ads have been shown to drive sales for local businesses. In fact, studies have found a 20% lift in sales for businesses that use connected TV ads, compared to those that don't. This is likely due to the high engagement and attention of viewers on streaming services.

### How do I target my audience with connected TV ads?

Connected TV ads allow you to target your audience based on demographics, interests, behaviors, and more. You can also use lookalike targeting to reach new customers who are similar to your existing customers. This level of targeting can help ensure that your ad is seen by people who are actually interested in your products or services.

### Do connected TV ads really outperform social media ads?

Yes, connected TV ads have been shown to outperform social media ads in terms of ad recall and sales lift. In fact, studies have found that 50% of ad spend is wasted on social media ads, whereas connected TV ads can drive a 200% increase in ad recall compared to digital ads. This makes connected TV ads a valuable option for local businesses looking to maximize their ad spend.
