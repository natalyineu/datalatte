## How Pandora Advertising Works for Local Business

Pandora Ad Studio lets you build a self-serve audio campaign with geographic, demographic, and interest-based (music genre, listening habits) targeting. Ads run as 15-30 second audio spots with an optional companion banner that displays during playback — useful for driving clicks even though the core format is audio-only.

**Geographic targeting** can be set down to a radius around your business address, making Pandora genuinely viable for single-location local businesses, not just national brands.

## Cost and Minimums

Pandora Ad Studio campaigns can start from roughly **$250-$500** for a focused local test, with CPMs typically running **$10-$18**. This is considerably more accessible than Pandora's legacy managed-service advertising, which historically required five-figure minimums through an ad agency relationship.

<Callout type="tip">
Start with a geo-targeted campaign limited to a 10-15 mile radius and a relevant music genre or two tied to your customer demographic — broad national targeting wastes budget for a single-location business.
</Callout>

## Who Pandora Works Best For

Local businesses with a strong audio-friendly use case — fitness studios (workout playlists), coffee shops and restaurants (commute-time listening), and auto service businesses (drive-time audiences) — tend to see the clearest fit. Pandora's commute-time listening peak (7-9am, 4-7pm) aligns naturally with "near me, on my way" intent for food, coffee, and auto services.

## Pandora vs Spotify: The Practical Difference

Pandora's self-serve platform has historically been more accessible to true local advertisers than Spotify Ad Studio, with lower practical minimums and more mature geo-targeting tools, though Spotify has closed much of that gap in recent years. Both are worth testing if your customer base skews toward streaming audio consumption.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does Pandora advertising cost for a small business?

Expect to spend $250-$500 for a meaningful local test, with CPMs around $10-$18. There's no longer a hard minimum that excludes small businesses from Pandora Ad Studio.

### Is Pandora advertising worth it for local businesses?

It depends on your audience's listening habits and your service category. Drive-time, commute-adjacent businesses (coffee, food, auto) tend to see the strongest fit.

### How does Pandora targeting work for local businesses?

You can target by geographic radius around your business, age, gender, and music genre/listening interest — giving single-location businesses meaningfully precise reach without national waste.
