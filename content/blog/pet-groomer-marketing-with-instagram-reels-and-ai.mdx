### How to Create Engaging Reels

So, what makes a Reel engaging? Here are some tips to get you started:

- **Keep it short**: Reels should be 60 seconds or less.
- **Use high-quality visuals**: Showcase your grooming skills, and use good lighting and sound.
- **Add captions and music**: Make your Reel accessible and enjoyable.
- **Include a CTA**: Encourage viewers to book an appointment or visit your website.

<Callout type="tip">Use Instagram's built-in features, such as polls and quizzes, to increase engagement and encourage audience participation.</Callout>

### Warning: Don't Rely on Spammy Tactics

While it's tempting to buy followers or use bots to artificially inflate engagement, these tactics can harm your account's credibility and even lead to account suspension. Focus on creating high-quality content that resonates with your audience.
