### The Consequences of Inconsistent NAP Citations
Inconsistent NAP citations lead to:
- Confusion for search engines and users
- Decreased online visibility
- Higher costs for attracting new customers

### Fixing Inconsistent NAP Citations: A Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Claim and Optimize Your Google My Business Listing
This is the first step in establishing a solid foundation for your local SEO. Ensure your listing is complete, accurate, and up-to-date.

### 2. Verify and Correct Your Listings in Online Directories
Use tools like Moz Local or BrightLocal to find and correct any inconsistencies in online directories like Yelp, Bing Places, and others.

### 3. Monitor and Update Your Listings Regularly
Schedule regular checks to ensure your NAP remains consistent across the web.
