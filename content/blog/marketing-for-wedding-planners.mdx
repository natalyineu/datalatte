As a wedding planner, you need a solid marketing strategy to build your reputation and fill your calendar. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

### Step 1: Claim Your Google Business Profile and Get Reviews

Your Google Business Profile is the first thing couples see when searching for wedding planners. Claiming and optimizing your profile is crucial. Research shows that 85% of couples trust online reviews, and 70% of them check reviews before making a decision.

<Callout type="coffee">I've worked with wedding planners who've seen a 30% increase in bookings after optimizing their Google Business Profile.</Callout>

To get more reviews, implement a review strategy:

- Provide exceptional service to every client
- Encourage happy clients to leave reviews on Google
- Respond promptly to all reviews, both positive and negative

### Step 2: Leverage Social Media to Showcase Your Work

Social media is a powerful tool for wedding planners. Share behind-the-scenes content, sneak peeks, and testimonials to build your brand's reputation. Research shows that 62% of couples follow wedding planners on social media, and 45% of them book a planner after seeing their work on Instagram.

Create a content calendar and post high-quality content regularly. Use Instagram Stories and IGTV to share exclusive content and engage with your audience.

### Step 3: Develop a Content Marketing Strategy

Content marketing helps you establish yourself as an expert in the wedding planning industry. Create informative blog posts, videos, and guides that provide value to your audience. Research shows that 85% of couples trust wedding planners who produce high-quality content.

<Callout type="tip">Invest in a content calendar tool to streamline your content creation and scheduling process.</Callout>

Identify your target audience and create content that resonates with them. Use SEO techniques to optimize your content for search engines.

### Step 4: Utilize Email Marketing

Email marketing is an effective way to nurture leads and build relationships with potential clients. Research shows that 70% of couples prefer email communication over social media.

<Callout type="example">I worked with a wedding planner who saw a 25% increase in bookings after implementing an email marketing strategy.</Callout>

Create a welcome email series that provides value to your subscribers. Use email automation tools to personalize your messages and nurture leads.
