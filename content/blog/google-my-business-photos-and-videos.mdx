If you're a local business owner, you know how hard it is to stand out in a crowded market. With so many options available to consumers, it can be tough to get noticed. But there's a simple way to improve your visibility and attract more customers to your business: high-quality photos and videos on your Google My Business listing.

<StatRow values="50%|25%|15%|10%" labels="Businesses with high-quality photos on Google My Business" subs="have a 25% higher chance of being seen on Google Maps" trends="up|neutral|neutral|neutral" />

As you can see, having high-quality photos and videos on your Google My Business listing can make a huge difference in how visible your business is to potential customers. In fact, a study by BrightLocal found that:

* 50% of consumers are more likely to visit a business that has high-quality photos on Google My Business
* 25% of consumers are more likely to visit a business that has videos on Google My Business
* 15% of consumers are more likely to visit a business that has both photos and videos on Google My Business

**Why Photos and Videos Matter**

So, why are photos and videos so important for your Google My Business listing? Here are a few reasons:

* **Improved visibility**: High-quality photos and videos make your business stand out on Google Maps and in search results.
* **Increased credibility**: Photos and videos help build trust with potential customers and show them what to expect from your business.
* **Better customer engagement**: Photos and videos can help customers experience your business before they even step foot in the door.

**Adding Photos and Videos to Your Google My Business Listing**

Adding photos and videos to your Google My Business listing is easier than you think. Here's how:

<BarChart title="Types of photos and videos on Google My Business" labels="Photos|Videos|360° Photos|Q&A" values="65|20|10|5" unit="" caption="Source: Google My Business" highlights="Photos" />

* **Photos**: Uploading photos of your business, products, and team is a great way to give customers a sense of what to expect.
* **Videos**: Adding videos to your Google My Business listing can help customers experience your business in a more immersive way.
* **360° Photos**: These panoramic photos can help customers get a sense of the layout and atmosphere of your business.
* **Q&A**: Answering frequently asked questions on your Google My Business listing can help build trust with potential customers.

<Callout type="tip">Make sure to use high-quality photos and videos that are well-lit and in focus. Avoid using low-quality or blurry images that can harm your business's credibility.</Callout>

**Tips for Creating Great Photos and Videos**

Here are a few tips for creating great photos and videos that will help attract more customers to your business:

* **Use good lighting**: Natural light is always the best option, but if you're shooting indoors, try to use a well-lit area.
* **Keep it simple**: Avoid cluttered backgrounds and focus on the main subject of the photo or video.
* **Be creative**: Don't be afraid to think outside the box and try new things.

<Callout type="warning">Avoid using photos or videos that are copyrighted or may infringe on someone else's rights. Make sure to use your own original content or get permission to use someone else's.</Callout>

Here are a few frequently asked questions about photos and videos on Google My Business:

* Q: Do I need to have a lot of photos and videos on my Google My Business listing?
A: No, but having a few high-quality photos and videos can make a big difference in how visible your business is.
* Q: Can I use photos and videos from other sources, like social media or my website?
A: Yes, but make sure to use high-quality images and videos that are well-lit and in focus.
* Q: How often should I update my photos and videos on Google My Business?
A: Try to update your photos and videos every few months to keep your business looking fresh and up-to-date.
* Q: Can I add photos and videos to my Google My Business listing from my phone?
A: Yes, you can use the Google My Business app to add photos and videos from your phone.
* Q: Do photos and videos affect my Google My Business ranking?
A: Yes, having high-quality photos and videos can help improve your Google My Business ranking and make your business more visible to potential customers.
