Iraq is one of the Arab world's largest economies — 42 million people, significant oil wealth, and a rapidly growing consumer economy driven by young demographics (median age ~22). Baghdad is the capital and largest city; Basra is the southern port and oil city; Erbil (Hewlêr) is the capital of the Kurdistan Region of Iraq — one of the Middle East's most rapidly developing cities with a distinct business and digital environment.

Iraq's digital marketing scene has been evolving rapidly. The Kurdistan Region (Erbil, Sulaymaniyah, Duhok) has a particularly developed commercial digital market. Facebook and Instagram are dominant; TikTok has exploded. The Iraqi Dinar (IQD) is the currency; USD is widely used in commerce.

## Iraq's Digital Platform Landscape

| Platform | Active Users (Iraq) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | 18M (43%) | Dominant social platform |
| YouTube | 22M (52%) | Very high — Arabic content popular |
| Instagram | 12M (29%) | Strong lifestyle and business |
| WhatsApp | 20M (48%) | Primary messaging |
| TikTok | 15M (36%) | Explosive growth |
| Telegram | 8M (19%) | Growing information platform |
| Google | 90%+ search share | Dominant |

**Facebook + TikTok powerful combination**: Iraq's youth demographic has made TikTok extraordinarily popular — Arabic content reaches millions organically. Facebook remains the primary business and community platform. Many Iraqi businesses run both.

**WhatsApp universal for business**: All business communication, ordering, and customer service in Iraq flows through WhatsApp. WhatsApp Business with Arabic and Kurdish language profiles is essential.

**Kurdistan Region distinct market**: Erbil and Sulaymaniyah have a more sophisticated and affluent consumer market than most of Iraq — driven by greater stability, strong business activity, and connections to Turkish, Gulf, and international markets. Kurdish language (Sorani dialect) is important in the KRI.

## Three Iraqi Business Examples

### 💇 Beauty Salon, Erbil

**Strategy**: Instagram beauty transformation content in Arabic and Kurdish (Sorani), Facebook for Erbil community groups, TikTok in Arabic, Google Maps, WhatsApp for bookings, Zain Cash for payments.

**Budget**: IQD 1,300,000/month (≈$1,000): IQD 540,000 Google Ads, IQD 480,000 Meta/TikTok Ads, IQD 280,000 content.

### ☕ Specialty Café, Baghdad Mansour

**Strategy**: Instagram with Baghdad café aesthetic in Arabic, TikTok content, Facebook for Baghdad community groups, Google Maps, WhatsApp for reservations.

**Budget**: IQD 1,040,000/month (≈$800): IQD 430,000 Google Ads, IQD 380,000 Meta/TikTok Ads, IQD 230,000 content.

### 🏋️ Gym, Baghdad/Erbil

**Strategy**: TikTok workout videos in Arabic (massive reach for zero cost), Instagram fitness content, Facebook page, Google Maps, WhatsApp for membership, Google Search Ads.

**Budget**: IQD 910,000/month (≈$700): IQD 380,000 Google Ads, IQD 340,000 Meta/TikTok Ads, IQD 190,000 content.

## Iraqi Marketing Calendar

| Period | Opportunity |
|---|---|
| March 21 | Nawruz (Kurdish/Persian New Year) — major in KRI |
| March 8 | International Women's Day |
| May | Eid al-Fitr (end of Ramadan — date varies) |
| Ramadan | Peak evening commercial activity |
| July | Eid al-Adha (date varies) |
| October 3 | Independence Day |
| November/December | End-of-year commercial |

**Nawruz (March 21)**: Kurdish New Year is the biggest celebration in the Kurdistan Region — three days of holiday with family gatherings, outdoor celebrations, and significant commercial activity. Beauty salons fully booked; restaurants packed. Start KRI campaigns March 10.

**Ramadan**: Iraq's Muslim majority makes Ramadan a major commercial period — evening iftar gatherings drive restaurant bookings, and the atmosphere of community creates strong social media engagement. Beauty salons see significant pre-Eid demand.

**Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha**: Both Eid holidays generate major commercial peaks — new clothes, beauty appointments, restaurant meals with extended family, and gift-giving. Start beauty salon campaigns 1 week before.

## FAQ

**Arabic or Kurdish for Iraq marketing?**
Arabic for Baghdad and central/southern Iraq. Kurdish (Sorani dialect, written in modified Arabic script) for Erbil, Sulaymaniyah, and the Kurdistan Region. The KRI is a distinct market — many businesses in Erbil produce separate Arabic and Kurdish content.

**Is Iraq safe for international digital marketing agency relationships?**
The Kurdistan Region (Erbil, Sulaymaniyah) is very stable and has attracted significant international investment. Baghdad has improved substantially. For most digital marketing purposes, Iraq is a viable and growing market. The Kurdistan Region in particular is often treated separately as a premium MENA market.
