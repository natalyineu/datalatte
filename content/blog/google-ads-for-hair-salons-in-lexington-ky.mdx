That bottom-of-funnel intent is what Google Ads captures at exactly the right moment.

## Lexington-Specific Strategy Tips

### Neighbourhood Targeting
Lexington customers search with strong local identity — they often include neighbourhood names in searches. Add Chevy Chase, Hamburg, Victorian Square as location modifiers in your ad copy and keyword variants to capture this intent. A resident of Chevy Chase is much more likely to click an ad that mentions their area than a generic "Lexington" ad.

### Google Business Profile First
Before scaling Google Ads spend in Lexington, make sure your Google Business Profile is fully optimised. In low-competition markets like this, your GBP star rating and review count directly affect your Ad Quality Score and your conversion rate once people click through. Aim for 50+ reviews and a 4.5+ rating before running significant ad spend.

### Negative Keywords That Waste Budget in Lexington
These terms attract clicks that almost never convert for Lexington hair salons:
- Job-related: "jobs", "hiring", "career", "hair salon salary"
- DIY/training: "how to", "course", "training", "certification"
- Other cities: surrounding city names outside your service radius
- Irrelevant modifiers: "franchise", "wholesale", "supplier"

## Match Types: What Works in Lexington

For a low-competition market like Lexington:

- **Exact match** on your top 3–5 converting keywords — full control, no waste
- **Phrase match** on neighbourhood + service combinations to capture local variants
- **Broad match** only for discovery after your exact/phrase campaigns are profitable

<Callout type="warning">
Avoid broad match on generic terms like "hair salon" or "hair" until you have 90+ days of conversion data. In low-competition markets, broad match without tight negative keyword lists can burn 40–60% of budget on irrelevant searches.
</Callout>

## Tracking What Matters

Google Ads in Lexington is only as good as what you can measure. Set up conversion tracking for:
1. **Phone calls** from ads (use Google's call tracking number)
2. **Booking form completions** on your website
3. **Direction requests** from your Google Business Profile
4. **"Book now" / contact page clicks** as a micro-conversion signal

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much should a hair salon in Lexington spend on Google Ads?

Most Lexington hair salons see meaningful results starting at **$800–$1,200/month**. At $3.40 average CPC, that delivers 100–150 targeted clicks from people actively searching for your service.

### Is Google Ads worth it for hair salons in Lexington?

Yes — especially for capturing bottom-of-funnel "near me" searches. In Lexington's market, the average hair salon customer found through Google Ads has a strong lifetime value of multiple visits, making the acquisition cost highly worthwhile once campaigns are dialled in.

### How long before I see results from Google Ads in Lexington?

Most Lexington hair salons see their first bookings from Google Ads within the first 2 weeks. The full optimisation cycle (where the algorithm learns your best customers) takes 60–90 days. Don't judge a campaign before 60 days of data.

### What's the biggest mistake hair salons make with Google Ads in Lexington?

Targeting the entire Lexington metro area instead of a tight radius around their location. Most hair salon customers won't travel more than 3–5 miles. Radius targeting around your address dramatically improves conversion rate and reduces wasted spend.
